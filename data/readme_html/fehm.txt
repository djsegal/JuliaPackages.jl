<p>FEHM.jl interfaces <a href="http://fehm.lanl.gov" rel="nofollow">FEHM</a>.</p>
<p>FEHM.jl is a module of the <a href="http://github.com/zemjulia">ZEM</a> framework.</p>
<p>ZEM is an integrated open-source high-performance computational (HPC) framework for environmental management in <a href="http://julialang.org" rel="nofollow">Julia</a>.</p>
<p>ZEM includes a series of modules (FEHM.jl, mysql.jl, etc.).</p>
<p>ZEM is coupled with <a href="http://mads.lanl.gov" rel="nofollow">Mads</a>.</p>
<p>ZEM has been tested to perform HPC simulations on a wide-range multi-processor clusters and parallel environments (Moab, Slurm, etc.).</p>
