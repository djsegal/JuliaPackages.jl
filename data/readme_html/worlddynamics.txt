<p dir="auto">The World Dynamics project aims to provide a modern framework to investigate <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Integrated_assessment_modelling" rel="nofollow">integrated assessment models</a> of sustainable development, based on current software engineering and scientific machine learning techniques. Our group is developing a Julia library to allow scientists to easily use and adapt different world models, from <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/World3" rel="nofollow">Meadows et al.'s World3</a> to recent proposals. By enabling an open, interdisciplinary, and consistent comparative approach to scientific model development, our goal is to inform global policy makers on environmental and economic issues.</p>
