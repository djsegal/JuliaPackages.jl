<p dir="auto">An implementation of the BSDESPOT (Better Selection DESPOT) online POMDP Solver. BSDESPOT is a variation of DESPOT. It provides action branch selection based on upper and lower bounds, and multi-observation branches selection.</p>

<p dir="auto"><math-renderer class="js-inline-math" style="display: inline" data-static-url="https://github.githubassets.com/static" data-run-id="4e668b0d17f15173f13be2108fdb8a97">$\zeta$</math-renderer> is the parameter to determine how close the branches are to the optimal ones will be selected. If you need to dynamically adjust <math-renderer class="js-inline-math" style="display: inline" data-static-url="https://github.githubassets.com/static" data-run-id="4e668b0d17f15173f13be2108fdb8a97">$\zeta$</math-renderer> during planning, please define a function related to d and k (d is the ratio of current depth to the maximum depth, and k means the ratio of remaining scenarios in current belief to <math-renderer class="js-inline-math" style="display: inline" data-static-url="https://github.githubassets.com/static" data-run-id="4e668b0d17f15173f13be2108fdb8a97">$K$</math-renderer>), i.e.</p>
