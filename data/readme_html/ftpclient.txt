<p dir="auto">Travis CI currently [does not reliably support FTP connections on sudo-enabled Linux]<a href="https://blog.travis-ci.com/2018-07-23-the-tale-of-ftp-at-travis-ci" rel="nofollow">https://blog.travis-ci.com/2018-07-23-the-tale-of-ftp-at-travis-ci</a>).
This will usually manifest itself as a <code>Connection Timeout</code> error. Disable <code>sudo</code> for a
workaround.</p>
