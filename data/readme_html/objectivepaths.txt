<p dir="auto">ObjectivePaths is a small Julia library aiming to make a few operations around handling paths to folders and files easier. It's a small wrapper around Base's <a href="https://docs.julialang.org/en/v1/base/file/" rel="nofollow">file system</a> applying some ideas from Python's <a href="https://docs.python.org/3/library/pathlib.html" rel="nofollow">pathlib</a> library, in a Julian way.</p>
