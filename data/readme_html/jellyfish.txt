<p dir="auto">Port of the <a href="https://github.com/sunlightlabs/jellyfish">jellyfish string comparison library</a>.</p>
<p dir="auto">All underlying functions in jellyfish are written in c, this packages just calls those functions directly.</p>
<p dir="auto">JellyFish.jl is subject to the BSD-3 license as per the original jellyfish module.</p>
