<div class="snippet-clipboard-content notranslate position-relative overflow-auto" data-snippet-clipboard-copy-content="    sf36(v::AbstractVector; version = :v1)"><pre class="notranslate"><code>    sf36(v::AbstractVector; version = :v1)
</code></pre></div>
<p dir="auto">Calculate SF36 results for vector of answers <code>v</code>.</p>
<p dir="auto">version keyword:</p>
<ul dir="auto">
<li><code>:v1</code> - General US population (Ware, John &amp; MA, Kosinski &amp; Keller, S.D.. (1993). SF-36 Physical and Mental Health Summary Scales: a User's Manual. 8. 23-28.)</li>
<li><code>:v2</code> - Correlated scores (Grassi, M. and Nucera, A. (2010), Dimensionality and Summary Measures of the SF-36 v1.6: Comparison of Scale- and Item-Based Approach Across ECRHS II Adults Population. Value in Health, 13: 469-478.)</li>
</ul>
<p dir="auto">No other available yet.</p>
<p dir="auto">Vector <code>v</code> mapping:</p>
<p dir="auto">1,33:36 - General Health(GH) - 1, 11a:11d</p>
<p dir="auto">3:12 - Physical functioning (PF) - 3a:3j</p>
<p dir="auto">13:16 - Role-Physical Functioning (RP) - 4a:4d</p>
<p dir="auto">17:19 - RoleEmotional (RE) - 5a:5c</p>
<p dir="auto">20,32 - Social Functioning (SF) - 6,10</p>
<p dir="auto">21,22 - Bodily pain (BP) - 7,8</p>
<p dir="auto">24:26,28,30 - Mental Health (MH) - 9b:9d, 9f, 9h</p>
<p dir="auto">23,27,29,31 - Vitality (VT) - 9a, 9e, 9g, 9i</p>
