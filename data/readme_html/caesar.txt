<p dir="auto">A multimodal/non-Gaussian robotic toolkit for localization and mapping -- reducing the barrier of entry for sensor/data fusion tasks, including Simultaneous Localization and Mapping (SLAM).</p>
<p dir="auto"><a href="http://www.navability.io" rel="nofollow">NavAbility.io</a> is administrating and supporting the ongoing development of Caesar.jl with and to help grow the community, please reach out for any additional information at <a href="mailto:info@navability.io">info@navability.io</a> or via the Slack badge-link below.</p>
