<p dir="auto">PGLib.jl provides easy access to the <a href="https://github.com/power-grid-lib/pglib-opf">Optimal Power Flow benchmark library</a> provided by the <a href="https://power-grid-lib.github.io/" rel="nofollow">IEEE PES Task Force on Benchmarks for Validation of Emerging Power System Algorithms</a>.</p>
