<p dir="auto">This package does histogram binning on a unit sphere, i.e. binning of unit vectors. It also provides some plotting utilities.</p>
<p dir="auto">Binning a single vector takes on the order of <code>25ns</code> for <code>10_000</code> bins and <code>35ns</code> for <code>1_000_000</code> bins on my machine.</p>
<hr>
<p dir="auto">Here's an example plot. The left side shows a histogram of 1e6 random unit vectors, sampled correctly. The right side shows <code>normalize(2rand(3) .- 1.0)</code>, also one million samples.</p>
