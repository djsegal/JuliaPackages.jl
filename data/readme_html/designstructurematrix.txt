<p dir="auto"><strong>D</strong>esign <strong>S</strong>tructure <strong>M</strong>atrix (<strong>DSM</strong>), also called <strong>D</strong>ependency <strong>S</strong>tructure <strong>M</strong>atrix, is a simple and useful method to represent complex system architectures or projects.</p>

<p dir="auto">This package, DesignStructureMatrix offers tools for displaying and analysing DSM.
For now, plotting DSM, clustering DSM and sequencing DSM are available.</p>
