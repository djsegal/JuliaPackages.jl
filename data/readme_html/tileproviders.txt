<p dir="auto">Provider map tiles in a <code>Provider</code> object.</p>
<p dir="auto">Complete urls to tiles can be retrieved with <code>geturl(provider::Provider, x::Int, y::Integer, z::Integer)</code>.</p>
<p dir="auto">A custom provider can be defined with, for example:</p>
<div class="highlight highlight-source-julia notranslate position-relative overflow-auto" dir="auto" data-snippet-clipboard-copy-content="using TileProviders
Provider(&quot;http://{s}.tile.openstreetmap.org/{z}/{x}/{y}.png&quot;)"><pre><span class="pl-k">using</span> TileProviders
<span class="pl-c1">Provider</span>(<span class="pl-s"><span class="pl-pds">"</span>http://{s}.tile.openstreetmap.org/{z}/{x}/{y}.png<span class="pl-pds">"</span></span>)</pre></div>

<p dir="auto">Some providers will need an <code>apikey</code>, <code>accesstoken</code>, <code>app_code</code> or <code>subscriptionkey</code> keyword if registration
is required to use the dataset. See the docs for the function.</p>
<p dir="auto">To get the specific urls from a <code>Provider</code> use:</p>
<div class="highlight highlight-source-julia notranslate position-relative overflow-auto" dir="auto" data-snippet-clipboard-copy-content="TileProviders.geturl(provider, x, y, z)"><pre>TileProviders<span class="pl-k">.</span><span class="pl-c1">geturl</span>(provider, x, y, z)</pre></div>
<p dir="auto">Get a dictionary of all <code>Providers</code> and their <code>variants</code> with:</p>
<div class="highlight highlight-source-julia notranslate position-relative overflow-auto" dir="auto" data-snippet-clipboard-copy-content="TileProviders.list_providers()"><pre>TileProviders<span class="pl-k">.</span><span class="pl-c1">list_providers</span>()</pre></div>
