<p>We recognized the need for an all-Julia, end-to-end tool that can</p>
<ul>
<li>Load multi-dimensional datasets quickly and incrementally.</li>
<li>Index the data and perform filter, aggregate, sort and join operations.</li>
<li>Save results and load them efficiently later.</li>
<li>Use Julia's built-in parallelism to fully utilize any machine or cluster.</li>
</ul>
<p>We built JuliaDB to fill this void.</p>
