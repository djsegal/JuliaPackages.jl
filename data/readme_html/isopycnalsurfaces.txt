<p dir="auto"><code>function vars2sigma(vars,p,p₀,σgrid;spline_order,linearinterp,eos)</code></p>
<p dir="auto">Input a Dictionary of variables (<code>vars</code>) that may have 1D or 3D arrays, <br>
a 1D vertical input coordinate (<code>p</code>), the reference pressure (<code>p₀</code>), <br>
the output vertical grid (<code>σgrid</code>). Optional keyword arguments are
the order of spline interpolation (<code>splorder</code>), a logical argument to force linear interpolation (<code>linearinterp</code>), and the equation of state type (<code>eos</code>). All variables are output to a Dictionary on isopycnal surfaces (<code>varsσ</code>).</p>

<ul dir="auto">
<li>"EOS80" from UNESCO 1980 (Saunders et al. 1980), following  gher-ulg/PhysOcean.jl</li>
<li>"JMD95" from Jackett and McDougall 1995, following gaelforget/MITgcmTools.jl</li>
<li>"TEOS10" from IOC et al. 2010, following GibbsSeaWater.jl</li>
</ul>
