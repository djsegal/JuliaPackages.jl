<p dir="auto">Based on orbit, spin, and 3-D shape, you can calculate the distribution of the surface temperature on an asteroid. The temperature distribution can be used to calculate the non-gravitational perturbations on its orbital and rotational motion (Yarkovsky and YORP effects, respectively).</p>

<ul dir="auto">
<li>1-dimensional heat diffusion in depth direction</li>
<li>Self-shadowing: Local shadows casted by topography</li>
<li>Self-heating: Re-absorption of scatterd and radiated photons by surrounding facets. Only sigle scattering is implemented.</li>
</ul>
