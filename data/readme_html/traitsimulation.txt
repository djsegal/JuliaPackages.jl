<p dir="auto">Authors: Sarah Ji, Chris German, Kenneth Lange, Janet Sinsheimer, Jin Zhou, Hua Zhou, Eric Sobel</p>
<p dir="auto">A convenient tool for simulating phenotypes for unrelateds or families under a variety of supported models.</p>
<p dir="auto">**(1) GLM Traits: Generalized Linear Models</p>
<p dir="auto">**(2) VCM Traits: Variance Component Models</p>
<p dir="auto">**(3) Improved Multinomial Case/Control Traits: Ordered Multinomial Models:</p>
<p dir="auto">**(4) GLMM Traits: Generalized Linear Mixed Models</p>
<p dir="auto"><em>Trait Simulation in Julia.</em></p>
