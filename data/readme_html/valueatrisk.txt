<p><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Value_at_risk" rel="nofollow">Value-at-Risk</a> is a method for measuring the risk of extremal losses in financial investments. <a href="https://doi.org/10.1016/j.ijforecast.2015.08.003" rel="nofollow">Nieto and Ruiz (2016)</a> and <a href="https://doi.org/10.1093/jjfinec/nbj002" rel="nofollow">Kuester et al. (2005)</a> provide a good overview of the subject.</p>
<p>This package is still at an early development stage. Please check the <a href="https://chm-von-tla.github.io/ValueAtRisk.jl/dev" rel="nofollow">documentation</a> for more.</p>
<p>Any feedback is appreciated!</p>
