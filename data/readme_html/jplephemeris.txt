<p dir="auto">The <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jet_Propulsion_Laboratory_Development_Ephemeris" rel="nofollow">JPL Development Ephemerides</a> are the results of simulations of the Solar System used for spacecraft navigation and astronomical purposes. They are published as <a href="http://naif.jpl.nasa.gov/pub/naif/generic_kernels/spk/" rel="nofollow">SPK kernel files</a> which contain sets of Chebyshev polynomial coefficients with which the position and velocity of the Solar System's planets can be interpolated with high precision for all dates covered by the ephemeris.</p>
<p dir="auto">This package provides functionality to read SPK files and compute the position and velocity of the planets directly from Julia.</p>
