<p dir="auto">The <em>FiberNlse.jl</em> package simulates the propagation of an optical field envelope signal of duration <strong>T</strong> in an optical fiber of length <em>L</em>. The chromatic dispersion (<strong>D</strong>) and SPM (self-phase modulation) (<strong>Î³</strong>) wich arises from <strong>Kerr</strong> non-linearity are taken as parameters.</p>
<p dir="auto">The core of the simulation consists in the integration of the <em>Non-Linear SchrÃ¶dinger Equation</em> with the desired signal as initial condition. The package uses the Fourier Split-Step Method algorithm.</p>
