<ul dir="auto">
<li>Riemann solver
- HLL
- HLLC
- Lax</li>
<li>Integrator
- Euler
- RK2
- RK3</li>
<li>Initial conditions
- 2D Kelvin-Helmholtz instability
- 1D and 2D sod shock tube</li>
<li>Boundary condition
- Transimissive
- Periodic</li>
</ul>
