<p><code>RecurrenceAnalysis</code>, which is one of the packages composing <strong>DynamicalSystems.jl</strong> (but can well be used as a stand-alone), offers tools for computing Recurrence Plots and exploring them within the framework of Recurrence Quantification Analysis.</p>
<p>For more details please see our detailed <a href="https://juliadynamics.github.io/DynamicalSystems.jl/latest/#recurrenceanalysis" rel="nofollow">documentation pages</a>.</p>
