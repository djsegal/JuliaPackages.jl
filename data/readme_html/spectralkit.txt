<p dir="auto">Building blocks of spectral methods for Julia. Currently includes Chebyshev polynomials on univariate and Smolyak (multivariate) grids, with domain transformations to semi-infinite and infinite domains.</p>

<p dir="auto">Mostly useful for algorithms along the lines of</p>
<blockquote>
<p dir="auto">Boyd, John P. <em>Chebyshev and Fourier spectral methods</em>. 2001.</p>
</blockquote>
<p dir="auto">The aim is to provide simple, well-tested, robust, and fast <em>building blocks</em> for spectral algorithms, which can be easily combined into algorithms.</p>
<p dir="auto">At the moment, the package API is experimental and subject to change.</p>
