<ul dir="auto">
<li><a href="/src/black">Implied Volatility Solver</a>: fast and robust solvers to find the Black-Scholes implied volatility corresponding to a given option price.</li>
<li><a href="/src/pln">Piecewise-Lognormal Model</a> (also known as spot model) for cash dividends: fast and accurate approximations.</li>
<li><a href="/src/american">American Option Pricing</a>: Andersen-Lake technique.</li>
<li><a href="/src/basket">Basket Option Pricing</a>: Curran approximations, which may also be reused to price Asian options or vanilla options in the piecewise-lognormal model.</li>
<li><a href="/src/collocation">Collocation</a>: fit market implied vols via stochastic collocation.</li>
<li><a href="/src/bachelier">Bachelier</a>: Bachelier formula and corresponding implied vol "solver".</li>
<li><a href="/src/random">Random Number Generators</a>: good pseudo and quasi random number generators.</li>
</ul>
