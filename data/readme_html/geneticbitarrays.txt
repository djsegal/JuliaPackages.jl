<p dir="auto">Minimal representations of DNA and RNA genetic sequences using <code>BitArray</code>s in <a href="https://julialang.org" rel="nofollow">Julia</a>.</p>

<p dir="auto">Input using <code>String</code>, <code>Vector{Char}</code> with nucleotides indicated by their <a href="https://www.bioinformatics.org/sms/iupac.html" rel="nofollow">IUPAC code</a>. You may also construct a sequence with a 4 x n <code>BitArray</code> - the same way sequences are represented internally with this package.</p>
