<p dir="auto">A small Julia package for the evaluation of <a href="https://dlmf.nist.gov/10#PT4" rel="nofollow">spherical Bessel
functions</a> and <a href="https://dlmf.nist.gov/33#PT2" rel="nofollow">Coulomb
functions</a> using the approach described
in</p>
<ul dir="auto">
<li>Barnett, A. R. (1996). The Calculation of Spherical Bessel and
Coulomb Functions. In (Eds.), Computational Atomic Physics
(pp. 181â€“202). Springer Berlin Heidelberg.</li>
</ul>
<p dir="auto">The article and accompanying codes (public domain) can be found at
<a href="http://www.fresco.org.uk/programs/barnett/index.htm" rel="nofollow">http://www.fresco.org.uk/programs/barnett/index.htm</a></p>
<p dir="auto">A fix in the rescaling step occurring in the vicinity of roots of the
<code>sinc</code> function was implemented similarly to the suggestion here:
<a href="https://github.com/manoharan-lab/holopy/pull/273/files">https://github.com/manoharan-lab/holopy/pull/273/files</a></p>
