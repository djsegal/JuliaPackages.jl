<p dir="auto">This package computes the synchrotron kernel for a frequency ratio <code>x = ŒΩ/ŒΩ0</code> and its polarisation components.</p>
<p dir="auto">It is based on <a href="https://github.com/grburgess/pynchrotron">pynchrotron</a> by J. Michael Burgess as used in <a href="https://www.nature.com/articles/s41550-019-0911-z?utm_source=feedburner&amp;utm_medium=feed&amp;utm_campaign=Feed%3A+natastron%2Frss%2Fcurrent+%28Nature+Astronomy%29&amp;utm_content=Google+Feedfetcher" rel="nofollow">Burgess et al (2019)</a> and is a re-implementation of the <a href="https://www.gnu.org/software/libc/" rel="nofollow">GSL</a> synchrotron functions.</p>
