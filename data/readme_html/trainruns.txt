<p dir="auto">TrainRuns.jl is a step towards open science and open data in railway engineering. Its modular design offers the possibility to serve as a basis for future optimization and development. TrainRuns.jl is suitable for qualitative calculations to compare different trains, and it is publicly available, and we invite others to collaborate.</p>
