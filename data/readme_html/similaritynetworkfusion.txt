<p>This is an implementation of the Similarity Network Fusion (SNF) method (<a href="https://www.nature.com/articles/nmeth.2810" rel="nofollow">Want et. al, Nature Methods 2014</a>) in Julia. This is not an official
implementation, as I'm not part of the authors of the paper. But, I followed
the implementation provided by the authors in R and translated it to Julia.</p>
