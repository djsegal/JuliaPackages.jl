<p dir="auto">The <strong>Number Line</strong> is a common teaching tool in e.g. K-12 math classes accross the U.S.</p>
<p dir="auto"><em>This package is in early development stage when breaking changes are to be expected.</em></p>

 <summary> Use Example </summary>
<p dir="auto">
</p><p dir="auto">Once you are looking at <code>standard_number_line.jl</code> via <code>Pluto.jl</code> in your web-browser (left image below), type a number of your choice in the text box and hit <code>add</code>.</p>
<p dir="auto">Repeat a few times, e.g. with other numbers, to obtain something like the image shown below on the right hand side. You can also hit <code>start over</code> at any point.</p>
