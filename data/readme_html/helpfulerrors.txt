<p dir="auto">Julia’s errors are much less helpful than they could (or should, as some would say) be. Let’s try to bridge the gap a bit.</p>
<p dir="auto">This package is a prototype for functionality that I think should be in the Base
  language. This isn’t a complete solution, but I think it’s a step in the right direction.</p>
