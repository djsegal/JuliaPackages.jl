<p dir="auto">This Julia package implements Uniformly Accurate numerical methods (UA) for highly oscillating problems. We propose to solve the following equation:</p>
<p dir="auto"><math-renderer class="js-display-math" style="display: block" data-static-url="https://github.githubassets.com/static" data-run-id="d61f2b2c1d7ba606f02303d1d286db03">$$\frac{d u(t)}{dt} = \frac{1}{\varepsilon} A u(t) + f(t, u(t)), \qquad u(t=t_{start})=u_{in}, \qquad \varepsilon \in ]0, 1],$$</math-renderer></p>
<p dir="auto">with</p>
<p dir="auto"><math-renderer class="js-inline-math" style="display: inline" data-static-url="https://github.githubassets.com/static" data-run-id="d61f2b2c1d7ba606f02303d1d286db03">$u : t \in [t_{start}, t_{end}] \mapsto u(t) \in \mathbb{R}^n, t_{start}, t_{end} \in \mathbb{R}, \qquad u_{in}\in \mathbb{R}^n$</math-renderer>,</p>
<p dir="auto"><math-renderer class="js-inline-math" style="display: inline" data-static-url="https://github.githubassets.com/static" data-run-id="d61f2b2c1d7ba606f02303d1d286db03">$A\in {\mathcal{M}}_{n,n}(\mathbb{R})\quad$</math-renderer> is such that <math-renderer class="js-inline-math" style="display: inline" data-static-url="https://github.githubassets.com/static" data-run-id="d61f2b2c1d7ba606f02303d1d286db03">$\quad \tau \mapsto e^{\tau A}\quad$</math-renderer> is <math-renderer class="js-inline-math" style="display: inline" data-static-url="https://github.githubassets.com/static" data-run-id="d61f2b2c1d7ba606f02303d1d286db03">$2 \pi$</math-renderer>-periodic,  <math-renderer class="js-inline-math" style="display: inline" data-static-url="https://github.githubassets.com/static" data-run-id="d61f2b2c1d7ba606f02303d1d286db03">$\quad f : (t, u) \in  \mathbb{R} \times \mathbb{R}^n \mapsto \mathbb{R}^n$</math-renderer>.</p>
