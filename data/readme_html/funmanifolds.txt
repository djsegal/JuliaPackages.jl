<p dir="auto">Functional differential geometry in Julia</p>
<p dir="auto">Features:</p>
<ul dir="auto">
<li>Expands upon the <a href="https://github.com/JuliaManifolds/Manifolds.jl"><code>Manifolds.jl</code></a> ecosystem.</li>
<li>Support for infinite dimensional manifolds, including
<ul dir="auto">
<li>spaces of curves on manifolds.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Differentiation of manifold-valued curves, including support for automatic differentiation.</li>
</ul>
<p dir="auto">Planned features:</p>
<ul dir="auto">
<li>More manifolds.</li>
<li>More transformations.</li>
<li>Square-root velocity function transformation (SRVF), including transported SRVF for curves on general manifolds.</li>
</ul>
