<p dir="auto">LocalOperators.jl is a simple package that exports a single <code>struct</code> type, <code>LocalOperator</code> representing an operator acting on a local Hilbert space. When adding, subtracting or multiplying two or more <code>LocalOperator</code> types together, the appropriate number of identity matrices are padded to each on the left and right such that they are compatible.</p>
