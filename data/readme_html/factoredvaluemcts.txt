<p dir="auto">This package implements the Monte Carlo Tree Search (MCTS) planning algorithm for Multi-Agent MDPs. The algorithm factorizes the true action value function, based on the locality of interactions between agents that is encoded with a Coordination Graph. We implement two schemes for coordinating the actions for the team of agents during the MCTS computations. The first is the iterative message-passing MaxPlus, while the second is the exact Variable Elimination. We thus get two different Factored Value MCTS algorithms, FV-MCTS-MaxPlus and FV-MCTS-VarEl respectively.</p>
<p dir="auto">The full FV-MCTS-MaxPlus algorithm is described in our AAMAS 2021 paper <em>Scalable Anytime Planning for Multi-Agent MDPs</em> (<a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/2101.04788" rel="nofollow">Arxiv</a>). The FV-MCTS-Varel is based on the Factored Statistics algorithm from the AAAI 2015 paper <em>Scalable Planning and Learning from Multi-Agent POMDPs</em> (<a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/1404.1140" rel="nofollow">Extended Version</a>) applied to Multi-Agent MDPs rather than POMDPs. We use the latter as a baseline and show how the former outperforms it on two distinct simulated domains.</p>
<p dir="auto">To use our solver, the domain must implement the interface from <a href="https://github.com/JuliaPOMDP/MultiAgentPOMDPs.jl">MultiAgentPOMDPs.jl</a>. For examples, please see <a href="https://github.com/JuliaPOMDP/MultiAgentSysAdmin.jl">MultiAgentSysAdmin</a> and <a href="https://github.com/JuliaPOMDP/MultiUAVDelivery.jl">MultiUAVDelivery</a>, which are the two domains from our AAMAS 2021 paper. Experiments from the paper are available at <a href="https://github.com/rejuvyesh/FVMCTS_experiments">https://github.com/rejuvyesh/FVMCTS_experiments</a>.</p>
