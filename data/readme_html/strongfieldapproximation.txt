<p dir="auto">This package provides some routines to calculate <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/High_harmonic_generation" rel="nofollow">high-order harmonic
  generation</a> (HHG) in the strong-field approximation (SFA), according
  to the Lewenstein model, presented in</p>
<ul dir="auto">
  <li>Lewenstein, M., Balcou, P., Ivanov, M. Y., Lâ€™Huillier, A., &amp; Corkum,
    P. B. (1994). Theory of high-harmonic generation by low-frequency
    laser fields. Physical Review A, 49(3),
    <ol dir="auto">
      <li><a href="http://dx.doi.org/10.1103/PhysRevA.49.2117" rel="nofollow">http://dx.doi.org/10.1103/PhysRevA.49.2117</a></li>
    </ol>
  </li>
</ul>
