<p dir="auto">In case where a function is equal to or can be approximated by a <strong>finite</strong> Spherical harmonic expansion</p>
 
<div align="center" dir="auto"><a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" href="https://render.githubusercontent.com/render/math?math=%5Csum_%7Bl%3D0%7D%5E%7BL%7D%5Csum_%7Bm%3D-l%7D%5El%20c_%7Bl%2Cm%7D%20r%5El%20Y_l%5Em%7B%5Cleft(%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Br%7D%5C%2C%20%5Cmathbf%20r%5Cright)%7D%2C"><img src="https://render.githubusercontent.com/render/math?math=%5Csum_%7Bl%3D0%7D%5E%7BL%7D%5Csum_%7Bm%3D-l%7D%5El%20c_%7Bl%2Cm%7D%20r%5El%20Y_l%5Em%7B%5Cleft(%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Br%7D%5C%2C%20%5Cmathbf%20r%5Cright)%7D%2C" style="max-width: 100%;"></a></div>
<p dir="auto">with  <a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" href="https://render.githubusercontent.com/render/math?math=L%20%5Cin%20%5Cmathbb%20N"><img src="https://render.githubusercontent.com/render/math?math=L%20%5Cin%20%5Cmathbb%20N" style="max-width: 100%;"></a> its multivariate polynomial representation has finite degree.</p>
<p dir="auto">Coefficents  <a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" href="https://render.githubusercontent.com/render/math?math=c_%7Bl%2Cm%7D"><img src="https://render.githubusercontent.com/render/math?math=c_%7Bl%2Cm%7D" style="max-width: 100%;"></a> can be initialized and populated by <code>c[l,m] = 42.0</code>.</p>
<div class="highlight highlight-source-julia notranslate position-relative overflow-auto" dir="auto" data-snippet-clipboard-copy-content="L = 2
