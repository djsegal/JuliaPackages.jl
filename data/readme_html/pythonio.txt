<p dir="auto">These most commonly used functions are exported from <code>PythonIO</code>:</p>
<ul dir="auto">
<li><code>readpyexpr(file; simplify=false)</code></li>
<li><code>readpkl(file; simplify=false)</code></li>
<li><code>readnpy(file)</code></li>
<li><code>writenpy(file, array)</code></li>
<li><code>readnpz(file, [key_or_keys])</code></li>
<li><code>writenpz(file, dict_of_arrays)</code></li>
</ul>
<p dir="auto">The <code>file</code> argument is an IO stream or filename.</p>
<p dir="auto">Further functionality is exported from sub-modules, described below.</p>
