<p dir="auto">Functions for generating various polynomial bases, and for generating sample points.
Functions generating a basis are have the prefix <code>bases</code>. Functions generating sample points have the prefix <code>sample_points</code>, the result is a vector of vectors containing the (1, 2 or <code>n</code> coordinates of the) sample points.</p>
