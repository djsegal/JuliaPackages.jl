<p>Dependency aware feature selection is a simple but effective method proposed in <em>Somol, Petr, Jiří Grim, and Pavel Pudil.
"Fast dependency-aware feature selection in very-high-dimensional pattern recognition."
2011 IEEE International Conference on Systems, Man, and Cybernetics. IEEE, 2011.</em>. The method is realated to Shapley values
(<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shapley_value" rel="nofollow">https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shapley_value</a>) and hence to explanation methods based on this GT concept
<em>Kononenko, Igor. "An efficient explanation of individual classifications using game theory." Journal of Machine Learning Research 11.Jan (2010): 1-18.</em></p>
