<p dir="auto">Fast implementation of the ClusterDepth multiple comparison algorithm from Frossard and Renaud <a href="https://doi.org/10.1016/j.neuroimage.2021.118824" rel="nofollow">Neuroimage 2022</a></p>
<p dir="auto">This is especially interesting to EEG signals. Currently only acts on a single channel/timeseries. Multichannel as discussed in the paper is the next step.</p>

<p dir="auto">Uncorrected should be 1 - it is very improbable, than none of the 200 tests in one repetition is not significant (we expect 5% to be).</p>
<p dir="auto">Corrected should be 0.05 (CI-95 [0.043,0.0564])</p>
<p dir="auto">* red noise introduces strong correlations between individual trials, thus makes the tests correlated while following the H0.</p>
