<p dir="auto">Yunir.jl is a lightweight toolkit for Arabic Natural Language Processing (ANLP). It offers APIs for the building blocks of ANLP specifically <em>dediacritization</em>, <em>normalization</em>, <em>transliteration</em> (including <em>custom transliteration</em>), <em>simple encoding</em>, and <em>orthographical analysis</em>.</p>
<p dir="auto">Yunir (ينير) /yunīr/ is the Arabic word for "illuminate." The logo is a Kufic calligraphy of the Arabic word نور /nūr/ or "light," specifically, a heatless light such as that of the light of the moon.</p>
