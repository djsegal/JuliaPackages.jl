<p dir="auto">This package combines the <code>IntervalArithmetic.jl</code> and <code>TaylorSeries.jl</code> packages to provide <strong>Taylor models</strong>, i.e.
Taylor polynomials with guaranteed error bounds to approximate functions.</p>
<p dir="auto">An introduction is available in <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=o1h7BUW04NI" rel="nofollow">this video from JuliaCon 2018</a>.</p>
