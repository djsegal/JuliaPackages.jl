<p dir="auto">This package serves as a portable deep learning toolbox for fusing physical and neural models. It's built upon <a href="https://github.com/FluxML/Flux.jl">Flux.jl</a>. The name of course comes from the 1972 film by Andrei Tarkovsky, which discusses the ultimate inadequacy of communication between human and non-human species.</p>
