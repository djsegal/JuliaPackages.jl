<p dir="auto">The package adds extra functionality on top of the <a href="https://github.com/JuliaInterop/MathLink.jl">MathLink</a> package, allowing Julia to talk to Mathematica.
For <a href="https://jupyter.org/" rel="nofollow">jupyter</a> notebooks, the text/latex MIME-type is implemented.</p>
