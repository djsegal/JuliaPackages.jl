<p>ImageFiltering implements blurring, sharpening, gradient computation,
and other linear filtering operations, as well nonlinear filters like
min/max.</p>
<p>Packages with overlapping functionality that also offer something extra:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="https://github.com/JuliaDSP/DSP.jl">DSP</a></li>
</ul>
<p>Packages that appear to perform just a subset of what ImageFiltering does:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="https://github.com/JeffreySarnoff/RollingFunctions.jl">RollingFunctions</a></li>
<li><a href="https://github.com/emmt/LocalFilters.jl">LocalFilters</a> (see <a href="https://github.com/JuliaImages/ImageMorphology.jl">ImageMorphology</a> for the morphological operations of JuliaImages)</li>
</ul>
