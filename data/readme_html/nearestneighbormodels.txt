<p dir="auto">Package providing K-nearest neighbor regressors and classifiers, for use with the <a href="https://alan-turing-institute.github.io/MLJ.jl/dev/" rel="nofollow">MLJ</a> machine learning framework.</p>
<p dir="auto">Builds on Kristoffer Carlsson's <a href="https://github.com/KristofferC/NearestNeighbors.jl">NearestNeighbors</a> package, for performing efficient nearest neighbor searches.</p>
<p dir="auto">Builds on contributions of Thibaut Lienart originally residing in <a href="https://github.com/JuliaAI/MLJModels.jl/blob/98618d7be53f72054de284fa1796c5292d9071bb/src/NearestNeighbors.jl#L1">MLJModels.jl</a>.</p>
<p dir="auto">Provides the following models: <code>KNNRegressor</code>, <code>KNNClassifier</code>,
<code>MultitargetKNNRegressor</code> and <code>MultitargetKNNClassifier</code>.</p>
<p dir="auto">Provides a library of kernels for weighting nearest neighbors, including
all kernels surveyed in the paper <a href="https://perun.pmf.uns.ac.rs/radovanovic/publications/2016-kais-knn-weighting.pdf" rel="nofollow">Geler et.al (2016):
Comparison of different weighting schemes for the kNN classifier on
time-series
data</a></p>
<p dir="auto">Do <code>list_kernels()</code> for a complete list.</p>
<p dir="auto">For instructions on defining a custom kernel, do <code>?UserDefinedKernel</code>.</p>
