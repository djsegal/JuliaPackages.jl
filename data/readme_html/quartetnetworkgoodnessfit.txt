<p><code>QuartetNetworkGoodnessFit</code> is a Julia package, providing tools to measure the
goodness of fit of a phylogenetic network to data on subsets of 4 tips.
It depends on the <a href="https://github.com/crsl4/PhyloNetworks.jl">PhyloNetworks</a>
package.</p>
