<p dir="auto">Implementation of special exponential families (currently in 1 dimension) and fitting through Lindsey's method.</p>
<blockquote>
<p dir="auto">Efron, B., &amp; Tibshirani, R. (1996). Using specially designed exponential families for density estimation. The Annals of Statistics, 24(6), 2431-2461.</p>
</blockquote>
<p dir="auto">Also implements estimation of exponential families in the empirical Bayes problem using regularized maximum likelihood.</p>
<blockquote>
<p dir="auto">Efron, B. "Empirical Bayes deconvolution estimates." Biometrika 103.1 (2016): 1-20.</p>
</blockquote>
<p dir="auto">The latter method is also available in the <a href="https://github.com/bnaras/deconvolveR">deconvolveR</a> R package.</p>
