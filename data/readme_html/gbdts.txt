<p dir="auto">Grammar-based decision tree (GBDT) is an interpretable machine learning model that can be used for the classification and categorization of heterogeneous multivariate time series data. GBDTs combine decision trees with a grammar framework. Each split of the decision tree is governed by a logical expression derived from a user-supplied grammar. The flexibility of the grammar framework enables GBDTs to be applied to a wide range of problems. In particular, GBDT has been previously applied to analyze multivariate heterogeneous time series data of failures in aircraft collision avoidance systems [1].</p>
<p dir="auto">[1] Lee et al. "Interpretable Categorization of Heterogeneous Time Series Data", preprint, 2018.</p>
