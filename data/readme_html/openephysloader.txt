<p>This package is tested against Julia <code>0.6</code>, <code>0.7</code>, <code>1.0</code>, <code>1.1</code>, and nightlies on Linux, OS X, and Windows.</p>
<p>This package only supports reading from continuous files at the moment, with no immediate
plans to support spike data.</p>
