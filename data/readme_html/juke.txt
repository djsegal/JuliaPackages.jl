<ul dir="auto">
<li>Parallel execution of external programs (similar to the <code>-j</code> flag of GNU Make).</li>
<li>Keep going unrelated jobs even when some jobs failes (similar to the <code>-k</code> flag of GNU Make).</li>
</ul>
