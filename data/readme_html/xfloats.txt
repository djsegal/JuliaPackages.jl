<p><code>XFloats</code> exports two these two types: <code>XFloat16</code>, <code>XFloat32</code>.</p>
<ul>
<li>These floating point types correspond to <code>Float16</code> and <code>Float32</code>.</li>
<li>Use them to compute results with greater accuracy at very high speed.</li>
</ul>
