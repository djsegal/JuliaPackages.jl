<p dir="auto">A Julia package for solving constrained trajectory optimization problems:</p>
<div class="snippet-clipboard-content notranslate position-relative overflow-auto" data-snippet-clipboard-copy-content="minimize        cost_T(state_T; parameter_T) + sum(cost_t(state_t, action_t; parameter_t))
states, actions
subject to      dynamics_t(state_t, action_t, state_t+1; parameter_t),         t = 1,...,T-1 
                constraint_t(state_t, action_t; parameter_t) {&lt;,=} 0,          t = 1,...,T
                state_lower_t &lt; state_t &lt; state_upper_t,                       t = 1,...,T 
                action_lower_t &lt; action_t &lt; action_upper_t,                    t = 1,...,T-1."><pre class="notranslate"><code>minimize        cost_T(state_T; parameter_T) + sum(cost_t(state_t, action_t; parameter_t))
states, actions
subject to      dynamics_t(state_t, action_t, state_t+1; parameter_t),         t = 1,...,T-1 
                constraint_t(state_t, action_t; parameter_t) {&lt;,=} 0,          t = 1,...,T
                state_lower_t &lt; state_t &lt; state_upper_t,                       t = 1,...,T 
                action_lower_t &lt; action_t &lt; action_upper_t,                    t = 1,...,T-1.
</code></pre></div>
<p dir="auto">with direct trajectory optimization.</p>
<p dir="auto">Fast and allocation-free gradients and sparse Jacobians are automatically generated using <a href="https://github.com/JuliaSymbolics/Symbolics.jl">Symbolics.jl</a> for user-provided costs, constraints, and dynamics. The problem is solved with <a href="https://coin-or.github.io/Ipopt/" rel="nofollow">Ipopt</a>.</p>
