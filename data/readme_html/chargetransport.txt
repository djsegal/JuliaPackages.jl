<p dir="auto"><code>ChargeTransport.jl</code> simulates charge transport in semiconductors. To this end, it discretizes
the semiconductor drift-diffusion equations via the Voronoi finite volume method as implemented in <a href="https://github.com/j-fu/VoronoiFVM.jl">VoronoiFVM.jl</a>.</p>

<ul dir="auto">
<li>heterostructures</li>
<li>1D, 2D and 3D simulations</li>
<li>stationary and transient simulations</li>
<li>IV curves and scan protocols</li>
<li>an arbitrary amount of charge carriers may be added</li>
<li>thermodynamically consistent, physics preserving numerical methods</li>
<li>different charge carrier statistics per species (Boltzmann, Blakemore, Fermi-Dirac)</li>
</ul>
