<p dir="auto">Julia wrappers for GDIP library written in C++. The provided optimal solution of the GDIP wchich enables to find a tight lower-bound for the Dubins Traveling Salesman Problem with Neighborhoods (DTSPN). This work was presented at RSS 2018 conference. Please, cite the journal version.</p>
