<p dir="auto">There is also <code>b2sin(θ)</code> for <code>θ ∈ [-π,π]</code>, and <code>Bhaskara.sin(θ)</code> for <code>θ ∈ 𝐑</code>,
by inserting appropriate <code>mod(,2π)</code> and <code>sign()*abs()</code> bits.
Similarly <code>bcos(θ)</code> is for <code>θ ∈ [-π/2,π/2]</code>, and <code>Bhaskara.cos(θ)</code> for all <code>θ ∈ 𝐑</code>.</p>
