<div id="readme" class="md" data-path="README.md"><article class="markdown-body entry-content container-lg" itemprop="text"><p dir="auto"><strong>MarketRisk.jl</strong> is a Julia library wich provides high-performance market risk measures for your portfolio. It currently supports:</p>
<ul dir="auto">
<li>
<p dir="auto"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Value_at_risk" rel="nofollow">Value at Risk (VaR)</a>: Most popular market risk measure which gives maximum possible loss given a confidence level and a time horizon.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Expected_shortfall" rel="nofollow">Expected Tail Loss (ETL or CVaR)</a>: A more complete risk measure than VaR.</p>
</li>
</ul>
