<p>This package intends to provide fast scattering transforms of images, sounds and videos.
Scattering coefficients are powerful features for signal-based machine learning tasks, as they offer a great discriminability
while discarding non-informative variability, such as rigid motions, frequency transpositions, and elastic deformations.</p>
<p>Its design is inspired from the <a href="https://github.com/lostanlen/scattering.m">scattering.m</a> toolbox, while taking advantage of
Julia's multiple dispatch and in-place operations.</p>
<p>The development of this package has been stopped and superseded by Kymatio, a PyTorch/Keras/NumPy implementation: <a href="https://github.com/kymatio/kymatio">https://github.com/kymatio/kymatio</a></p>
