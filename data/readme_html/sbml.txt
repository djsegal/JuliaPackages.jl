<p dir="auto">This is a simple wrap of some of the libSBML functionality, mainly the model loading for purposes of COBRA analysis methods and exploration of ODE system and reaction dynamics.</p>
<p dir="auto">You might like to try the packages that use SBML.jl; these now include:</p>
<ul dir="auto">
<li><a href="https://github.com/LCSB-BioCore/COBREXA.jl">COBREXA.jl</a>, the exascale-ready
constraint-based analysis and reconstruction toolkit for finding and modeling
steady metabolic fluxes with the models</li>
<li><a href="https://github.com/SciML/SBMLToolkit.jl">SBMLToolkit.jl</a>, for working with
the reaction dynamics of the models as ODE systems, well connected to the
<a href="https://github.com/SciML">SciML</a>
<a href="https://github.com/SciML/ModelingToolkit.jl">ModelingToolkit</a> ecosystem.</li>
</ul>
<p dir="auto">Other functionality will be added as needed. Feel free to submit a PR that increases the loading "coverage".</p>
