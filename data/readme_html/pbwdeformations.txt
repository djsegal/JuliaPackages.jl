<p dir="auto">The package will provide both a general framework and specialized functions in order to</p>
<ul dir="auto">
<li>classify PBW deformations of certain smash products and</li>
<li>study their representations.</li>
</ul>
<p dir="auto">To solve classification problems efficiently, we use representation theoretic ideas.</p>
