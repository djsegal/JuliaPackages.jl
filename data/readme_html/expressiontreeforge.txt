<p dir="auto">ExpressionTreeForge.jl is a manipulator of expression trees.
It supports several expression tree implementations and defines methods to analyze and manipulate them, including:</p>
<ul dir="auto">
<li>partial separability detection;</li>
<li>evaluation of the expression, and its first and second derivatives;</li>
<li>bound propagation;</li>
<li>convexity detection.</li>
</ul>
