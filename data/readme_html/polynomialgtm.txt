<p dir="auto">NASA Langley has developed a scaled-down radio-controlled aircraft called the
Generic Transport Model (GTM). This model is physically similar to a typical
commercial plane (think 737, etc.), and is used for off-nominal flight control
research.
<a href="https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0967066110002595" rel="nofollow">Chakraborty et al</a>
published a polynomial approximation for longitudinal GTM dynamics (near select
flight conditions). Joe Carpinelli (me!) wrote these dynamics in Python, and
<a href="https://github.com/cadojo/Replicated-ROA-Analysis">replicated and summarized</a>
parts of Chakraborty et al's region of attraction analysis for the
polynomial-approximated GTM dynamics.</p>
<p dir="auto">This Julia package extends
<a href="https://github.com/SciML/ModelingToolkit.jl">ModelingToolkit.jl</a> to provide
this polynomial-approximated model of longitudinal GTM flight dynamics to the
Julia ecosystem!</p>
