<p dir="auto">A Julia installation v1.3 or higher is required.
To get the newest version of this package, Julia v1.6 LTS or newer is recommended.</p>
<p dir="auto">Magnitude and Phase images in NIfTI fileformat
(4D images with echoes in the 4th dimension, 5D images with channels in the 5th dimension)</p>
