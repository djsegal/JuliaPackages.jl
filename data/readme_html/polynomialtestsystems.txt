<p dir="auto"><a href="https://github.com/JuliaHomotopyContinuation/PolynomialTestSystems.jl">PolynomialTestSystems.jl</a> is a collection of systems of polynomials for benchmarking, testing etc.</p>
<p dir="auto">Some of the polynomial systems are obtained from the database of polynomial
systems created by Jan Verschelde and available under <a href="http://homepages.math.uic.edu/~jan/" rel="nofollow">http://homepages.math.uic.edu/~jan/</a>.</p>
