<p dir="auto">If you have questions about Ferrite.jl you're welcome to reach out to us on the Julia Slack
under <code>#ferrite-fem</code> or on Zulip under <code>#Ferrite.jl</code> (<a href="https://julialang.org/community/" rel="nofollow">links to Slack and
Zulip</a>). Alternatively you can start a <a href="https://github.com/Ferrite-FEM/Ferrite.jl/discussions/new">new discussion</a> in the
discussion forum on the repository. Feel free to ask us even if you are not sure the problem
is with Ferrite.jl.</p>
<p dir="auto">If you encounter what you think is a bug please report it, see
<a href="CONTRIBUTING.md#reporting-issues">CONTRIBUTING.md</a> for more information.</p>
