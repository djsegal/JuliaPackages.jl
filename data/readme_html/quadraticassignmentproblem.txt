<p>Algorithms for (approximately) solving the quadratic assignment problem (QAP) implemented in Julia.</p>
<p>Exact algorithms:</p>
<ul>
<li>Quadratic integer programming</li>
<li>Linearization</li>
</ul>
<p>Approximation algorithms:</p>
<ul>
<li>"On the Maximum Quadratic Assignment Problem" by Nagarajan and Sviridenko (NS)</li>
<li>"Maximum Quadratic Assignment Problem: Reduction from Maximum Label Cover and LP-based Approximation Algorithm" by Makarychev, Manokaran, and Sviridenko (MMS)</li>
</ul>
<p>Heuristic algorithms:</p>
<ul>
<li>Fast Approximate QAP</li>
<li>Random search</li>
<li>LP + LAP rounding</li>
</ul>
