<p dir="auto">This "package" is a prototype for various operations needed for a next-generation package cache in Julia,
targeted to be merged in the Julia 1.9 and/or 1.10 timeframe. Many of these operations are currently
performed in Julia's <code>dump.c</code> (and perhaps soon, <code>staticdata.c</code>); at present it seems possible that at
least a subset of this could be written in Julia and move into <code>Core</code>. If that isn't viable, then
at least this package may be useful for planning implementations.</p>
<p dir="auto">Features that are implemented or in-progress include:</p>
<ul dir="auto">
<li>handling of backedges and their validation in the user's environment</li>
