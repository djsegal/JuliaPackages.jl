<p dir="auto">Official Implementation of ICML 2023 Paper <a href="https://arxiv.org/pdf/2303.02262.pdf" rel="nofollow">Locally Regularized Neural Differential Equations: Some Black Boxes Were Meant to Remain Closed!</a></p>
<p dir="auto">Extension of <a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/2105.03918" rel="nofollow">RegNeuralDE</a> to allow for local
regularization.</p>
<p dir="auto">This is a research repository. Most users should wait for the functionality to be available
through packages like <code>DiffEqFlux.jl</code>.</p>

<ul dir="auto">
<li>
<p dir="auto">(Randomized) Local Regularization of Neural ODEs yield similar trajectories to global
regularization.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto">Local Regularization is simpler to implement since it doesn’t require
discretize-then-optimize. Rather it can utilize the more commonly used
optimize-then-discretize approach.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto">Additionally introduces benefits for cases where the “blackbox cannot be opened” entirely
(like in diffusion models)</p>
</li>
</ul>
