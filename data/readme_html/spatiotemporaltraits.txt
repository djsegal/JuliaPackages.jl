<p dir="auto"><code>SpatioTemporalTraits</code> serves as a relatively low-level source of spatiotemporal traits, allowing other packages the opportunity to use a common interface for their unique types.
Many of these traits take advantage of <a href="https://github.com/JuliaArrays/ArrayInterface.jl">ArrayInterface.jl</a> and have sensible defaults for subtypes of <code>AbstractArray</code> or any other type that uses <code>ArrayInterface</code>.</p>
