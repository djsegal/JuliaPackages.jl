<p dir="auto">This package is a growing collection of Lie Group/Algebra, Quaternion, Euler, AxisAngle representations. Including convert functions between each, and overloading operators for those sets. The package already includes exponential, logarithm maps and Jacobians for Lie SO(3). SE(3) mostly complete.</p>
