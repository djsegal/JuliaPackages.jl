<p>The input must be convertible to UTF-8. Byte sequences that represent an invalid UTF-8 string will be rejected, per spec.</p>
<p>The TOML types are converted to their natural Julia counterparts (except datetimes, see below). Arrays are typed.</p>
<p>The parser is strict, and will throw a <code>TOMLError</code> on malformed input.</p>

<p>To keep the dependencies low (the Calendar package is very slow to
load), and waiting for the implemetation of <code>Timestamp</code>s in the <code>Base</code> Julia library, TOML <code>DateTime</code>s are
currently converted to <code>TOML.DateTime</code> objects.</p>
