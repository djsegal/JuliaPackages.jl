<p dir="auto">The goal of <em>GraphInvariants.jl</em> is to provide a wide range of functions for computing invariants on graphs, and is inspired by the <a href="https://github.com/somacdivad/grinpy">GrinPy</a> Python library.</p>
<p dir="auto"><em>GraphInvariants.jl</em> computes invariants for any graph type that implements the <a href="https://github.com/JuliaGraphs/Graphs.jl">Graphs.jl</a> <code>AbstractGraph</code> interface.</p>
