<p dir="auto">Algorithms for the optimization of Julia expressions derived from a grammar.</p>
<p dir="auto">The following algorithms are implemented:</p>
<ul dir="auto">
<li>Monte Carlo</li>
<li>Genetic Programming</li>
<li>Grammatical Evolution</li>
<li>Cross-Entropy Method</li>
<li>Probabilistic Incremental Program Execution (PIPE)</li>
</ul>
