<p dir="auto">This package utilises JuMP to define a two-dimensional parametric representation of the dual solution of a linear programme,
as a function of the two right-hand side values. If you use this package for academic work, please cite this paper:</p>
<p dir="auto">M. Habibian et al. Co-optimization of demand response and interruptible load reserve offers for a price-making major
consumer. Energy Systems, 11, pp. 45-71, (2020).</p>
