<p dir="auto">This is a suite for analyzing calibration of probabilistic predictive models written in Julia.</p>
<p dir="auto"><strong>It is available for use in Julia, <a href="https://github.com/devmotion/pycalibration">Python</a>, and <a href="https://github.com/devmotion/rcalibration">R</a>.</strong></p>
<p dir="auto">The package supports:</p>
<ul dir="auto">
<li><a href="https://github.com/devmotion/ReliabilityDiagrams.jl">plotting reliability diagrams</a></li>
<li><a href="https://github.com/devmotion/CalibrationErrors.jl">estimating calibration errors</a></li>
<li><a href="https://github.com/devmotion/CalibrationTests.jl">performing calibration tests</a></li>
</ul>
