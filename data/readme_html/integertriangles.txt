<p>We give a framework for computing mathematical integer triangles and use
it to create "Integer Triangle Trait Cards"â„¢. A trait card is a compilation of the essential characteristics of an integer triangle, whereby we understand the characteristics of a triangle to be integer sequences that can be obtained from the triangle by elementary transformations.</p>
<p>To see what you can expect start by executing</p>
<pre><code>using IntegerTriangles
dim = 8
TraitCard(BinomialTriangle, BinomialTransform, dim)
</code></pre>
<p>You can also look at the demo <a href="https://github.com/OpenLibMathSeq/IntegerTriangles.jl/blob/master/demos/IntegerTriangles.ipynb">notebook</a>.</p>
