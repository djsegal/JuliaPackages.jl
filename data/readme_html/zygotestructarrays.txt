<p dir="auto">Defines <a href="https://github.com/FluxML/Zygote.jl">Zygote</a> adjoint rules for <a href="https://github.com/JuliaArrays/StructArrays.jl">StructArrays</a>.</p>

<p dir="auto">Install with <code>] add ZygoteStructArrays</code>. Then simply <code>import ZygoteStructArrays</code> and the adjoint rules will just work.</p>
