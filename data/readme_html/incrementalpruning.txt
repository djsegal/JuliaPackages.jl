<p dir="auto">This solver implements the incremental pruning algorithm as described in Zhang and Liu (1996) and Cassandra et al. (1997). This solution method is exact (ϵ-optimal) but is much slower than modern approximate solution techniques. As such, it is only computationally feasible for small problems.</p>
