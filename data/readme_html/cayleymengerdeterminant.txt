<p dir="auto">Calculate the Cayley-Menger squared-distance matrix of an <code>N</code>-simplex from its points, and use the matrix’s determinant to compute the (interior) measure of the simplex—for example, the area of a triangle, or the volume of a tetrahedron.</p>
<p dir="auto">This package exports two small utility functions, <code>binomial2</code> and <code>inverse_binomial2</code>, as well as the full-featured immutable structured matrix type <code>CayleyMengerDistanceMatrix</code> and its constructors, and the function <code>simplex_volume</code> for calculating the measure directly from the points.</p>
