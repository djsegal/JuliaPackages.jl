<p><strong>IndividualDisplacements.jl</strong> computes point displacements over a gridded domain. It is geared towards the analysis of Climate, Ocean, etc models (<code>Arakawa C-grids</code> are natively supported) and the simulation of material transports within the Earth System (e.g. plastics or planktons in the Ocean; dusts or chemicals in the Atmosphere).</p>
<p>Inter-operability with popular climate model grids via <a href="https://github.com/JuliaClimate/MeshArrays.jl">MeshArrays.jl</a> is an important aspect. The package can read and write individual displacement collection files, including those generated by the <a href="https://mitgcm.readthedocs.io/en/latest/?badge=latest" rel="nofollow">MIT general circulation model</a>. <code>IndividualDisplacements</code>'s initial test suite is based on global ocean model simulations called <a href="https://eccov4.readthedocs.io/en/latest/" rel="nofollow">ECCO (v4r2)</a> and <a href="https://cbiomes.readthedocs.io/en/latest/" rel="nofollow">CBIOMES (alpha)</a> (see <a href="https://doi.org/10.5194/gmd-8-3071-2015" rel="nofollow">Forget et al. 2015</a>).</p>
