<p dir="auto">A Julia package for molecular dynamics trajectories analysis and modeling of biomolecules. Note that this package is continually under development.</p>
<p dir="auto">Some functions have been ported into Julia from our old MATLAB/Octave toolbox <a href="https://github.com/ymatsunaga/mdtoolbox">https://github.com/ymatsunaga/mdtoolbox</a></p>
<p dir="auto">Julia version 1.6 or later is required.</p>
