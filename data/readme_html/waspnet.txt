<p dir="auto"><code>WaspNet.jl</code> is a Julia package for fixed-time-step simulations of primarily spiking neural networks (SNNs).</p>
<p dir="auto"><code>WaspNet.jl</code> is intended for exploring SNN or hybrid architectures and experimenting with new neuron models. <code>Network</code> and <code>Layer</code> abstractions are provided, and an <code>AbstractNeuron</code> type is defined for users to construct their own neuron models. Utilizing multiple dispatch ensures that new neuron types slot easily into the existing framework.</p>

<p dir="auto"><code>WaspNet.jl</code> is named after <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Megaphragma_mymaripenne" rel="nofollow">Megaphragma mymaripenne</a>, the third-smallest known extant insect and a microscopically sized wasp. <em>M. mymaripenne</em> are known for possessing a very low number of neurons (roughtly 7,000) yet they are still capabable of exhibiting higher order behavior.</p>
