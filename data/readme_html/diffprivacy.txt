<p dir="auto">A library to implement Differential Privacy techniques over statistical databases in Julia</p>
<p dir="auto">DiffPrivacy.jl makes it easier to implement noise in statistical databases to perform a sensitive query.
<br>
This library brings both Julia's crytographic community and statistics community together.</p>
