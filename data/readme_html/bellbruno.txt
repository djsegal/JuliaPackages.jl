<p dir="auto">Compute <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bell_polynomials" rel="nofollow">Bell polynomials</a> for <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fa%C3%A0_di_Bruno's_formula" rel="nofollow">Fa√† di Bruno's formula</a>.</p>
<p dir="auto"><strong>Basic usage</strong></p>
<div class="highlight highlight-source-julia notranslate position-relative overflow-auto" dir="auto" data-snippet-clipboard-copy-content="N_der = 10;              # Maximum order of Bell polynomial
