<p>Once <code>ImageIO</code> is installed, usage is as simple as:</p>
<div class="highlight highlight-source-julia position-relative" data-snippet-clipboard-copy-content="using FileIO

img = load(&quot;img.png&quot;)
"><pre><span class="pl-k">using</span> FileIO
<span class="pl-c1">save</span>(<span class="pl-s"><span class="pl-pds">"</span>img.png<span class="pl-pds">"</span></span>, <span class="pl-c1">rand</span>(Gray, <span class="pl-c1">100</span>, <span class="pl-c1">100</span>))

<div class="highlight highlight-source-julia position-relative" data-snippet-clipboard-copy-content="using PNGFiles
