<p dir="auto">For an introduction to the algorithms and an overview of the features, you can take a look at the blog posts: <a href="https://nextjournal.com/dgan181/julia-soc-19-quantum-algorithms-for-differential-equations/" rel="nofollow">#1</a>, <a href="https://nextjournal.com/dgan181/jsoc-19-non-linear-differential-equation-solver-and-simulating-of-the-wave-equation/" rel="nofollow">#2</a>.</p>
