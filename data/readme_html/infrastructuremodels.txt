<p>InfrastructureModels.jl encompasses shared functionalities, best practices, and style guides for a multi-infrastructure modeling and optimization ecosystem in Julia. The core packages in the InfrastructureModels ecosystem are</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="https://github.com/lanl-ansi/GasModels.jl">GasModels.jl</a> - Natural gas transmission systems</li>
<li><a href="https://github.com/lanl-ansi/PetroleumModels.jl">PetroleumModels.jl</a> - Petroleum product transmission systems</li>
<li><a href="https://github.com/lanl-ansi/PowerModels.jl">PowerModels.jl</a> - Electrical power transmission systems</li>
<li><a href="https://github.com/lanl-ansi/PowerModelsDistribution.jl">PowerModelsDistribution.jl</a> - Electrical power distribution systems</li>
<li><a href="https://github.com/lanl-ansi/WaterModels.jl">WaterModels.jl</a> - Potable water distribution systems</li>
</ul>
