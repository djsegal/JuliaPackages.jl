<p dir="auto">InfrastructureModels encompasses shared functionalities, best practices, and style guides for a multi-infrastructure modeling and optimization ecosystem in Julia. The core packages in the InfrastructureModels ecosystem are,</p>
<ul dir="auto">
<li><a href="https://github.com/lanl-ansi/GasModels.jl">GasModels</a> - Natural gas transmission systems</li>
<li><a href="https://github.com/lanl-ansi/PetroleumModels.jl">PetroleumModels</a> - Petroleum product transmission systems</li>
<li><a href="https://github.com/lanl-ansi/PowerModels.jl">PowerModels</a> - Electrical power transmission systems</li>
<li><a href="https://github.com/lanl-ansi/PowerModelsDistribution.jl">PowerModelsDistribution</a> - Electrical power distribution systems</li>
<li><a href="https://github.com/lanl-ansi/WaterModels.jl">WaterModels</a> - Potable water distribution systems</li>
</ul>
<p dir="auto">Additionally, the following multi-infrastructure modeling packages have been developed,</p>
<ul dir="auto">
<li><a href="https://github.com/lanl-ansi/GasPowerModels.jl">GasPowerModels</a> - Natural gas and electrical power transmission systems</li>
<li><a href="https://github.com/lanl-ansi/PowerWaterModels.jl">PowerWaterModels</a> - Electrical power and potable water distribution systems</li>
<li><a href="https://github.com/lanl-ansi/PowerModelsITD.jl">PowerModelsITD</a> - Electrical power transmission and distribution</li>
</ul>
<p dir="auto">For information about developing infrastructure modeling packages for new infrastructure types or new combinations of existing infrastructure types, please see the <a href="https://lanl-ansi.github.io/InfrastructureModels.jl/stable/developer/" rel="nofollow">developer section</a> of the package documentation.</p>
