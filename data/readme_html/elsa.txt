<p>A work-in-progress package for building discrete lattices and implementing arbitrary tight-binding models on them. This aims to be a successor of <a href="http://www.icmm.csic.es/sanjose/MathQ/MathQ.html" rel="nofollow">MathQ</a>, with a better design, better extensibility and superior functionality and performance.</p>
