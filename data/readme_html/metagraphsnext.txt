<p dir="auto">Welcome to MetaGraphsNext.jl, a type-stable replacement for <a href="https://github.com/JuliaGraphs/MetaGraphs.jl">MetaGraphs.jl</a>. It allows you to create graphs with vertex and edge metadata, on which you can unleash the full power of the <a href="https://github.com/JuliaGraphs/Graphs.jl">Graphs.jl</a> ecosystem.</p>
