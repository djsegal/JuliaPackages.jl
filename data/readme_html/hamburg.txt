<p dir="auto">Since July 27, 2020:</p>
<p dir="auto"><a href="https://www.hamburg.de/corona-zahlen" rel="nofollow">hamburg.de</a></p>
<p dir="auto">Since August 10, the number of hospitalized patients from this source is also being stored.</p>
<p dir="auto">Before July 27, 2020:</p>
<p dir="auto"><a href="https://www.rki.de/DE/Content/InfAZ/N/Neuartiges_Coronavirus/Situationsberichte/Gesamt.html" rel="nofollow">Robert Koch Institut</a></p>
<p dir="auto">The number of deaths are not quite matching up between those two datasources, it seems that the RKI is reporting around 30 more people who died through COVID-19.</p>
<p dir="auto">The numbers reported to the RKI are also delayed by one day, the time of recording has been moved back one day to align with the dates from hamburg.de.</p>

<p dir="auto">Number of people infected by covid-19, recorded since July 27, 2020</p>
<p dir="auto"><code>dataset("covid-19", "infected")</code></p>
<p dir="auto"><strong>Columns</strong>:</p>
<ul dir="auto">
<li><strong>deaths</strong>: Total number of deaths due to COVID-19</li>
<li><strong>new</strong>: New confirmed cases</li>
<li><strong>recordedat</strong>: Date of measurement</li>
<li><strong>recovered</strong>: Number of people recovered (not a precise number since people are not required to report recoveries)</li>
<li><strong>total</strong>: Total number of confirmed cases</li>
<li><strong>hospitalizations</strong>: Total number of hospitalized patients (due to covid 19), including the patients in intensive care</li>
<li><strong>intensivecare</strong>: Number of patients in intensive care due to covid 19</li>
<li><strong>first_vaccination</strong>: Number of people who received the first shot of the vaccine</li>
<li><strong>second_vaccination</strong>: Number of people who received the second shot of the vaccine</li>
</ul>
