<p>Have you ever created a work around because of a specific dependency version?
This package is a test utility to ensure you remember to fix your workaround after support for it has been dropped.</p>
<p>Whenever you create a workaround and plan to remove it after you drop version support for a package create a new test like so:</p>
<div class="highlight highlight-source-julia position-relative" data-snippet-clipboard-copy-content="using FixYourWorkaround

@test package_compatible(&quot;Package&quot;, &quot;Version&quot;)
"><pre><span class="pl-k">using</span> FixYourWorkaround

<span class="pl-c1">@test</span> <span class="pl-c1">package_compatible</span>(<span class="pl-s"><span class="pl-pds">"</span>Package<span class="pl-pds">"</span></span>, <span class="pl-s"><span class="pl-pds">"</span>Version<span class="pl-pds">"</span></span>)</pre></div>
<p>In the future when you remove the version from the <code>compat</code> section of your Project.toml this test will fail and remind you to remove your workaround.</p>
