<p dir="auto">International Standard Atmospheric (ISA) model</p>
<p dir="auto">Only metric units are used.</p>
<p dir="auto">Functions</p>
<ul dir="auto">
<li>
<p dir="auto">T_K     Atmospheric temperature [K]</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto">p_Pa    Air pressure [Pa]</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto">ρ_kg_m³ Air density [kg/m³]</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto">a_m_s   Speed of sound [m/s]</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto">Vcas2Vtas True airspeed Vtas [m/s] as a function of the calibrated airspeed Vcas [m/s]</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto">Vtas2Vcas Calibrated airspeed Vcas [m/s] as a function of the True airspeed Vtas [m/s]</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto">M2Vtas  True airspeed Vtas [m/s] as a function of the Mach number</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto">Vtas2M  Mach as a function of the True airspeed Vtas [m/s]</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto">M2Vcas  Calibrated airspeed Vcas [m/s] as a function of the Mach number</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto">Vtas2M  Mach as a function of the True airspeed Vtas [m/s]</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto">Hp_trans_m Transition altitude [m]</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto">conditions returns AtmosConditions</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto">θ   temperature ratio</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto">δ   pressure ratio</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto">σ   density ratio</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p dir="auto">Constants</p>
<ul dir="auto">
<li>T₀_K    Standard atmospheric temperature [K] at Mean Sea Level (MSL)</li>
<li>p₀_Pa   Standard atmospheric pressure [Pa] at Mean Sea Level (MSL)</li>
<li>ρ₀_kg_m³  Standard atmospheric density [kg/m³] at Mean Sea Level (MSL)</li>
<li>a₀_m_s  Speed of sound [m/s] at Mean Sea Level (MSL)</li>
<li>g₀_m_s² Graviation acceleration [m/s²]</li>
<li>Hp_trop_m Geopotential pressure altitude [m] of Tropopause</li>
</ul>
<p dir="auto">Type</p>
<ul dir="auto">
<li>AtmosConditions: Hp_m, T_K, p_Pa, ρ_kg_m³, a_m_s</li>
</ul>
