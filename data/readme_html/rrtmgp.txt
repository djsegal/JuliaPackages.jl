<p>RRTMGP.jl is registered in the general Julia registry. To install, enter the package manager by typing <code>]</code> in the Julia REPL, and then type:</p>
<div class="highlight highlight-source-julia"><pre>pkg<span class="pl-k">&gt;</span> add RRTMGP</pre></div>
<p>Then, to use</p>
<div class="highlight highlight-source-julia"><pre>julia<span class="pl-k">&gt;</span> <span class="pl-k">using</span> RRTMGP</pre></div>
