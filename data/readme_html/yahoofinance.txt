<p dir="auto"><a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" href="docs/src/assets/logo.png"><img src="docs/src/assets/logo.png" alt="&quot;logo&quot;" style="max-width: 100%;"></a></p>
<p dir="auto"><code>YahooFinance.jl</code> offers the possibility to access the Yahoo Finance market data in Julia.
This package is available in Python as <a href="https://github.com/markushhh/pyahoofinance/">pyahoofinance</a> and in R as <a href="https://github.com/markushhh/YahooFinance/">YahooFinance</a>.
It was inspired by <a href="https://cran.r-project.org/web/packages/quantmod/quantmod.pdf" title="Documentation" rel="nofollow">quantmod in R</a> but differs in its method to download the data.</p>
