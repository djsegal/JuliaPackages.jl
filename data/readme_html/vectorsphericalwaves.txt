<p dir="auto">Julia code to calculate vector spherical waves (M and N), which are solutions for vector wave equation.</p>
<p dir="auto">Equations and convention for vector spherical waves is following:
Mishchenko, M.I., Travis, L.D., and Lacis, A.A. (2002). Scattering, absorption, and emission of light by small particles (Cambridge University Press).</p>
