<p dir="auto"><a href="https://github.com/JuliaMath/Quadmath.jl/actions"><img src="https://github.com/JuliaMath/Quadmath.jl/workflows/CI/badge.svg" alt="GitHub CI Build Status" style="max-width: 100%;"></a></p>
<p dir="auto">This is a Julia interface to libquadmath, providing a <code>Float128</code> type corresponding to the IEEE754 binary128 floating point format.</p>

<p dir="auto">Quadmath currently works on x86_64 Linux, macOS, and Windows.</p>
<ul dir="auto">
<li>It may require a new-ish version of gcc which supports <code>__float128</code> type.</li>
<li>It has not been tested on 32 bit Linux.</li>
<li>It does not work on ARM due to the lack of libquadmath support for that platform.</li>
</ul>
