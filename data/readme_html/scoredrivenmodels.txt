<p>ScoreDrivenModels.jl is a Julia package for modeling, forecasting, and simulating time series with score-driven models, also known as generalized autoregressive score models (GAS). Implementations are based on the paper <a href="http://dx.doi.org/10.1002/jae.1279" rel="nofollow">Generalized Autoregressive Models with Applications</a> by D. Creal, S. J. Koopman, and A. Lucas.</p>
