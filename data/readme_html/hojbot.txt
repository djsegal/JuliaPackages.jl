<ol dir="auto">
<li>
<p dir="auto">The <em>happy reactor</em>: automatically reacts to your message with a smiley when your message sounds happy. Similarly, there are also <em>excited</em> and <em>disappointed</em> reactors.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto">The time zone command (<code>tz</code>): easily converts a date/time into multiple predefined timezones. This is perfect for setting up meeting time that works for our global community.
<a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" href="images/demo.gif"><img src="images/demo.gif" alt="tz demo" data-animated-image="" style="max-width: 100%;"></a></p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto">The julia command (<code>j</code>): return the docstring for a given name, statistics about the queried names, and the list of names in a given package.</p>
</li>
</ol>
