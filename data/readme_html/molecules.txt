<p dir="auto">This package offers tools to create and manipulate atomic ensamble for molecular simulations.</p>
<ul dir="auto">
<li>Parsing XYZ data</li>
<li>Manipulation of atomic/geometric structure</li>
<li>Properties such as center of mass, nuclear repulsion, nuclear dipoles and moments of inertia.</li>
</ul>
