<ol>
<li>Types and constructors for representing quantum states and operators.</li>
<li>Methods for evolving quantum systems and performing quantum measurements.</li>
<li>Methods for computing information-theoretic quantities.</li>
<li>Mathematics utilities to support quantum calculations.</li>
</ol>
