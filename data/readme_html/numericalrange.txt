<p dir="auto">Provides the Julia function</p>
<div class="snippet-clipboard-content notranslate position-relative overflow-auto" data-snippet-clipboard-copy-content="    nrange(A; nk = 1, thmax = 32, noplot = false)"><pre class="notranslate"><code>    nrange(A; nk = 1, thmax = 32, noplot = false)
</code></pre></div>
<p dir="auto">which evaluates and plots the numerical range of the
<code>nk</code> largest leading principal submatrices of <code>A</code>, using <code>thmax</code>
equally spaced angles in the complex plane.
The defaults are <code>nk = 1</code> and <code>thmax = 32</code>.
The eigenvalues of <code>A</code> are plotted as <code>x</code>.  The function returns <code>f</code> and <code>e</code>,
where <code>f</code> is the numerical range and <code>e</code> is a vector of eigenvalues of <code>A</code>.
Setting <code>noplot = true</code> suppresses the plot.</p>
<p dir="auto">This function is a direct translation of <code>fv.m</code> in Professor Nick Higham's <a href="https://www.mathworks.com/matlabcentral/fileexchange/2360-the-matrix-computation-toolbox" rel="nofollow">Matrix Computation Toolbox</a>,
based on an original routine by A. Ruhe.</p>
