<p dir="auto">A package for describing crystal orientations and transforming between their representations consistently. The basis for this package can be found in the tutorial:</p>
<p dir="auto">"Rowenhorst, David, et al. "Consistent representations of and conversions between 3D rotations." Modelling and Simulation in Materials Science and Engineering 23.8 (2015): 083501. <a href="https://iopscience.iop.org/article/10.1088/0965-0393/23/8/083501" rel="nofollow">https://iopscience.iop.org/article/10.1088/0965-0393/23/8/083501</a>".</p>

<p dir="auto">The package includes crystal orientation representations for:</p>
<ol dir="auto">
<li>Euler Angles
<ul dir="auto">
<li>Bunge</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Quaternions</li>
<li>Axis-Angle types
<ul dir="auto">
<li>Axis-Angle</li>
<li>Rodrigues-Frank vector</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>
<p dir="auto">As an example, one can create an Euler Angles representation of the Bunge (ZXZ) convention via:</p>
<p dir="auto"><code>euls = EulerAngles(Bunge, 0.0, 1.0, 0.3)</code></p>
