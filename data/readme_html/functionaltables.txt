<p dir="auto">Julia package for working with (potentially large) columns of data.</p>

<p dir="auto">A <em>table</em> is a collection of <em>columns</em>, indexed by <code>Symbol</code>s.</p>
<p dir="auto">Columns are <em>immutable</em>, which allows compression and type narrowing when applicable. Columns do not support random access, just <code>iterate</code>.</p>
<p dir="auto">Columns are created by collecting elements into <em>sinks</em>, which are then finalized. While being collected into, sinks can change representation, eg decide whether to use RLE or other compression schemes, <code>mmap</code> to disk for large data, etc --- these can be configured and ideally ignored by the user.</p>
<p dir="auto"><code>NamedTuple</code>s are used pervasively throughout the interface.</p>
