<p dir="auto">This package exports <code>StaticList</code> a statically-sized list.
Similar to Julia's <code>Tuple</code>, these types are useful for creating collections of small strongly-typed values.
Unlike <code>Tuple</code>, <code>StaticList</code> may be composed iteratively without having to reconstruct new instances.
This allows small collections (a length of a little over 32) to undergo many operations that may add or remove a few values with little overhead.
Inference is explicitly tested for most methods.
Most methods have also been bench marked using <code>BenchmarkTools</code> to ensure there's little to no overhead where possible.</p>
