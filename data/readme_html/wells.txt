<ul dir="auto">
<li>Wells.jl is a Julia code for multi-well variable-rate pumping test analysis based on analytical methods.</li>
<li>Wells.jl computes drawdown in confined, unconfined and leaky aquifers through a variety of analytical solutions.</li>
<li>Wells.jl considers fully or partially penetrating pumping and observation wells. It also includes wellbore storage capacity of pumping wells.</li>
<li>Wells.jl can simulate variable-rate pumping tests where the variable rate is approximated either as piecewise linear or as step changes. It can also handle standard exponential and sinusoidal changes in pumping rates.</li>
<li>Wells.jl utilizes the principle of superposition to account for transients in the pumping regime and to include multiple sources/sinks (e.g. pumping wells).</li>
<li>Wells.jl combines the use of the principle of superposition and method of images to represent constant head or no flow boundaries.</li>
</ul>
<p dir="auto">Wells.jl is a Mads module.</p>

MADS can execute a wide range of data- and model-based analyses:</p>
