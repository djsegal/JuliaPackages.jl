<p>Fire up a julia interactive window.</p>
<p>In the window press the ']' key.</p>
<p>You should observe the prompt change from <code>julia&gt;</code> to <code>pkg&gt;</code>.</p>
<p>Type in <code>add SpeedDate</code> and hit enter.</p>
<p>Press backspace and you should see the prompt change back to <code>julia&gt;</code>.</p>
<p>That's it!</p>
