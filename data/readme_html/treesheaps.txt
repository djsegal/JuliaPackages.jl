<p>Implementation of various tree and heaps (to do) structures. Only binary search tree, AVL tree, splay tree are supported now. Available operations includes <code>search</code>, <code>insert!</code>, <code>topdowninsert!</code>, <code>delete!</code>, <code>topdowndelete!</code>, <code>splay!</code>, <code>topdownsplay!</code>, <code>findmin</code>, <code>findmax</code>. Outputs are formatted through <code>AbstractTrees.jl</code>. Other structures and plotting recipe are under developments!</p>
