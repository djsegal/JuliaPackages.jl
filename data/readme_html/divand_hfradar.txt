<p dir="auto">The package <code>DIVAnd_HFRadar</code> interpolates surface current data on a regular grid possibly taking dynamical contraints into account.
The primary use-case is for radial current measurements for high-frequency radars (like WERA or CODAR SeaSonde). But it can also be applied to any other
current data (like ADCPs or drifters).</p>
<p dir="auto">The method is described in: Barth, A., Troupin, C., Reyes, E., Alvera-Azcárate, A., Beckers J.-M. and Tintoré J. (2021): <a href="https://doi.org/10.1007/s10236-020-01432-x" rel="nofollow">Variational interpolation of high-frequency radar surface currents using DIVAnd</a>. Ocean Dynamics, 71, 293–308
doi: 10.1007/s10236-020-01432-x (open access, <a href="CITATION.bib">bibtex</a>)</p>
