<ul dir="auto">
<li>Terrain filters, such as Progressive Morphological Filters (PMF, SMF) and Skewness balancing</li>
<li>Geospatial cost (friction) operations that mimic PCRaster. These functions should however be more Julian, extensible and scale better.</li>
<li>Visualization, such as Perceptually Shaded Slope Map (PSSM)</li>
<li>Terrain analysis functions, such as slope, aspect, roughness, Topographic Position Index (TPI), Terrain Ruggedness Index (TRI), curvature and hillslope.</li>
</ul>
