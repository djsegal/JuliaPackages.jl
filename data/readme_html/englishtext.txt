<p>Currently this package includes six features:</p>
<ul>
<li><code>indefinite</code> for finding the right indefinite article to use</li>
<li><code>english</code> for converting a positive integer to an English-language wordy
representation, and the inverse function <code>unenglish</code> to convert the wordy
representation back.</li>
<li><code>pluralize</code> to pluralize a noun, and the inverse function <code>singularize</code></li>
<li><code>ItemList</code> to create a list of objects</li>
<li><code>ItemQuantity</code> to associate a noun with a number</li>
<li><code>sentences</code> to iterate over the sentences of a text</li>
</ul>
<p>See the documentation (links above) for how to use these features.</p>
