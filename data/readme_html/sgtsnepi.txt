<p dir="auto">We provide a <code>Julia</code> interface, i.e., a wrapper to <a href="http://t-sne-pi.cs.duke.edu" rel="nofollow">SG-t-SNE-Π</a>, which
is a high-performance software for swift embedding of a large, sparse
graph into a d-dimensional space (d = 1,2,3) on a shared-memory
computer.</p>
