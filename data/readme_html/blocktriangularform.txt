<p dir="auto">This package provides a direct translation to Julia of the SuiteSparse BTF library. <code>BlockTriangularForm.order</code> and <code>BlockTriangularForm.btf</code> return permutations of a sparse matrix into block triangular form.</p>
<p dir="auto">Documentation and performance work are on-going, currently BlockTriangularForm is ~2x slower than BTF.</p>
