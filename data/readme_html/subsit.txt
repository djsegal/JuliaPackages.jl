<p dir="auto">Subspace iteration algorithm for the generalized eigenvalue problem of free
vibration.</p>
<p dir="auto">Implementation of the Bathe Subspace Iteration algorithms from the textbook
Finite Element Procedures (1996),  Table 11.3.</p>
<p dir="auto">A couple of twists have been added, which seem to improve convergence.
This package is sometimes slower than Arpack, but generally only 10-20%.
Other times it can be faster than  Arpack.</p>
<p dir="auto">With contributions of Michael Stewart.</p>
