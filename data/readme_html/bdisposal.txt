<p dir="auto">The BDisposal package proposes a serie of environmental efficiency and productivity algorithms for non-parametric modelling when we relax the disposability assumption of some of the outputs and/or inputs (e.g. pollution). These efficiency and productivity measures are implemented through convex and non-convex <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Data_envelopment_analysis" rel="nofollow">Data Envelopment Analysis (DEA)</a> (aka <em>Frontier Efficiency Analysis</em>) models.</p>
