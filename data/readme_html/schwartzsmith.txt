<p>SchwartzSmith.jl is a package for estimating the Schwartz Smith model.<br>
The implementation and notation was based on the original article by Eduardo Schwartz and James E. Smith (2000), called "Short-Term Variations and Long-Term Dynamics in Commodity Prices" (<a href="https://pdfs.semanticscholar.org/c298/e5c8c477e3da8941173e6cb593f05230006c.pdf" rel="nofollow">https://pdfs.semanticscholar.org/c298/e5c8c477e3da8941173e6cb593f05230006c.pdf</a>)</p>
<p>To use the package you can do</p>
