<p>Tools for reading (and eventually, perhaps, writing) CITI files. The <a href="http://literature.cdn.keysight.com/litweb/pdf/ads15/cktsim/ck2016.html" rel="nofollow">CITI file format</a> is commonly used in microwave engineering, for storing measured or computed <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scattering_parameters" rel="nofollow">scattering parameters</a> of multiport networks.</p>
