<p dir="auto">Hierarchical solvers is an approximate sparse direct solver, entirely written in Julia. It can run in superlinear complexity as approximate solver employing lowrank and hierarchically structured low-rank (HSS) matrices to compress Gauss transforms and Schur complements.</p>
