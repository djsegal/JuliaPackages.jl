<p dir="auto">User API:
<code>D(f)(x)</code> returns a lazy representation of the derivative.</p>
<p dir="auto"><code>D(f)(x) * v</code> computes <code>df(x)/dx ⋅ v</code>, taking advantage of the laziness in <code>D(f)(x)</code>.</p>
<p dir="auto"><code>DI(f)(x)</code> is a convenience function to materialize the derivative, gradient or
Jacobian of <code>f</code> at <code>x</code>.</p>
