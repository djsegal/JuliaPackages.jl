<ul dir="auto">
<li>
<p dir="auto">Easy to build both simple and complex tasks.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto">Supports external command lines and pure Julia functions.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto">Supports <strong>resuming</strong> interrupted tasks, <strong>retrying</strong> failed tasks, and <strong>skipping</strong> finished tasks.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto">Supports dependency check.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto">Supports inputs, outputs validation, and so on.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto">Supports program queuing and workload management with <a href="https://github.com/cihga39871/JobSchedulers.jl">JobSchedulers.jl</a></p>
</li>
</ul>
