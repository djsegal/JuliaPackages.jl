<p dir="auto">An extension to <a href="https://github.com/JuliaIntervals/IntervalArithmetic.jl">IntervalArithmetic.jl</a> with interval unions. Interval unions are sets defined by unions of disjoint intervals.</p>
<p dir="auto">Conversation in <a href="https://github.com/JuliaIntervals/IntervalArithmetic.jl/pull/452" data-hovercard-type="pull_request" data-hovercard-url="/JuliaIntervals/IntervalArithmetic.jl/pull/452/hovercard">PR#452</a></p>
<p dir="auto">This package includes constructors, arithmetic (including with intervals and scalars) and complement functions.</p>
