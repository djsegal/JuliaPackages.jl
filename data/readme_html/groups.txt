<p>A very rudimentary implementation of finitely-presented groups (syllable representation). Relatively complete are only <a href="https://github.com/kalmarek/Groups.jl/blob/master/src/AutGroup.jl">automorphism groups of free groups</a> and <a href="https://github.com/kalmarek/Groups.jl/blob/master/src/WreathProducts.jl">wreath products</a> (which are not finitely-presented, but based on the standard normal form).</p>
<p>Have a look into <code>test</code> directory for eample use.</p>
