<p dir="auto">A <a href="https://github.com/JuliaData/Tables.jl">Tables.jl</a>-based implementation of the VOTable standard. This allows simply accessing VOTables with Tables.jl sinks, like <code>DataFrame</code> from <a href="https::/github.com/JuliaData/DataFrames.jl">DataFrames.jl</a>.</p>
<p dir="auto"><strong>WARNING:</strong>
This package is fairly unpolished, and I (mileslucas) don't have a ton of time for developing it. Please use at your own caution, and I would be very eager for contributions. The end goal is to have a good suite of tools for accessing SIMBAD and Vizier, which requires a reliable VOTable parser.</p>
