<p dir="auto">Package providing the <code>StructTypes.StructType</code> trait for Julia types to declare the kind of "struct" they are,
providing serialization/deserialization packages patterns and strategies to automatically construct objects. To
learn more, check out the documentation links below.</p>
<p dir="auto"><strong>Installation</strong>: at the Julia REPL, <code>import Pkg; Pkg.add("StructTypes")</code></p>
<p dir="auto"><strong>Maintenance</strong>: StructTypes is maintained collectively by the <a href="https://github.com/orgs/JuliaData/people">JuliaData collaborators</a>.
Responsiveness to pull requests and issues can vary, depending on the availability of key collaborators.</p>
