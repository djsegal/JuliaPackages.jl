<p>KeyedDistributions.jl provides thin wrappers of <a href="https://juliastats.org/Distributions.jl/latest/types/#Distributions" rel="nofollow"><code>Distribution</code></a> and <a href="https://juliastats.org/Distributions.jl/latest/types/#Sampleable" rel="nofollow"><code>Sampleable</code></a>, to store keys for the variates.</p>

julia&gt; kd = KeyedDistribution(MvNormal(3, 1.0), [:x, :y, :z]);

([:x, :y, :z],)
