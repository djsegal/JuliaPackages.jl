<ul>
<li>Simple interface.</li>
<li>Fast implementation, unrolling when it makes sense.</li>
<li>Targeted to applications in statistics, mainly <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maximum_likelihood" rel="nofollow">ML</a>, <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maximum_a_posteriori_estimation" rel="nofollow">MAP</a>, and Bayesian inference.</li>
<li>Take advantage of Julia v0.7 features, especially named tuples and compiler optimizations.</li>
</ul>
