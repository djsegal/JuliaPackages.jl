<p>As quantum computation implementation in experiments grows rapidly, a toolkit for bridging the gap between experimentalist and theorists could be necessary. To be specific, Translating quantum algorithm to quantum circuits or Hamiltonians in other models with a lower resource cost is important for both experimentalists and theorists.</p>
<p>This project will help scientists to design better architecture of quantum computers in real life.</p>
