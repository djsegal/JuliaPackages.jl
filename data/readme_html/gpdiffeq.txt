<p dir="auto">This package is intended to connect Gaussian Processes and differential equations in a similar fashion to <a href="https://github.com/SciML/DiffEqFlux.jl">DiffEqFlux.jl</a>.</p>
<p dir="auto">It is the successor of <a href="https://github.com/Crown421/GaussianProcessODEs.jl">GaussianProcessODEs.jl</a>, which contains mostly original implementations and minimally documented research code. By contrast the <code>GPDiffEq</code> packages aims to take full advantage of the broader <a href="https://juliagaussianprocesses.github.io/" rel="nofollow">JuliaGaussianProcesses</a> eco system.</p>
