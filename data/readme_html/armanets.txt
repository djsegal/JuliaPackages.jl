<p>This is an implementation of ARMA layers and ARMA networks in Julia, based on the paper <a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/2002.11609" rel="nofollow">ARMA Nets: Expanding Receptive Field for Dense Prediction</a> (Su, Jiahao; Wang, Shiqi; Huang, Furong). The layers are a drop-in replacement (or addition) for standard convolutional layers.</p>
<p>See the <a href="https://tmthyln.github.io/ARMANets.jl/latest/" rel="nofollow">documentation</a> for more details.</p>
