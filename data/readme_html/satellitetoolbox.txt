<p>This package contains several functions to build simulations related with
satellites. It is used on a daily basis on projects at the <a href="http://www.inpe.br" rel="nofollow">Brazilian National
Institute for Space Research (INPE)</a>, and it is the engine
of the <a href="https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/abs/10.1177/1063293X18804006" rel="nofollow">Forplan Satellite Simulator</a>.</p>
