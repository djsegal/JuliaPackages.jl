<p dir="auto">This package shall implement different models of ground stations for airborne
wind energy system. A ground station has the following components:</p>
<ul dir="auto">
<li>motor/generator</li>
<li>gearbox (optional)</li>
<li>drum</li>
</ul>
<p dir="auto">Currently implemented is a model of the 20kW ground station from Delft University of Technology.</p>
