<p dir="auto"><code>KadanoffBaym.jl</code> is an <em>adaptive</em> solver for Kadanoff-Baym equations written in Julia. To learn more about the solver and Kadanoff-Baym equations, have a look into our <a href="https://doi.org/10.21468/SciPostPhysCore.5.2.030" rel="nofollow">accompanying paper</a>.</p>
