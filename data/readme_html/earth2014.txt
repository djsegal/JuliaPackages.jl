<div class="highlight highlight-source-julia"><pre>julia<span class="pl-k">&gt;</span> <span class="pl-k">using</span> Earth2014

<p>This will download the data via Datadeps.jl only once and make it available for future use.</p>
<p>Then you can use it for your research, or just plot it for example:</p>
<div class="highlight highlight-source-julia"><pre>julia<span class="pl-k">&gt;</span> <span class="pl-k">using</span> Plots
