<p dir="auto">This is a package to download KaTeX and make it available from Julia.</p>
<p dir="auto">The files <code>"auto-render.min.js", "katex.min.css", "katex.min.js"</code> are in the folder <code>KaTeX.assetsdir</code>, whereas KaTeX fonts are in <code>KaTeX.fontsdir = joinpath(KaTeX.assetsdir, "fonts")</code>.</p>
