<p dir="auto">use any of <code>(이),(가),(과),(와),(을),(를),(은),(는),(으로),(로),(아),(야),(이여),(여),(이라),(라)</code> inside korean sentence.
And <code>KoreanJosa.jl</code> will choose appropriate korean josa.</p>
