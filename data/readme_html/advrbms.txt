<p dir="auto">Train and sample <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Restricted_Boltzmann_machine" rel="nofollow">Restricted Boltzmann Machines</a> (<strong>RBMs</strong>) in Julia, with 1st and 2nd order <strong>adv</strong>ersarial constraints on the weights, that promote concentratation of information about labeled features into selected hidden units.</p>
