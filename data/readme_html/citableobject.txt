<p dir="auto">A Julia module for working with citable objects identified by <code>Cite2Urn</code>.</p>
<p dir="auto">The <code>Cite2Urn</code> implements the <code>Urn</code> abstraction (from the <a href="https://cite-architecture.github.io/CitableBase.jl/stable/" rel="nofollow">CitableBase module</a>.  (<a href="https://cite-architecture.github.io/CitableText.jl/stable/" rel="nofollow"><code>CitableText.jl</code></a> is a parallel module for working with citable passages of texts identified by <code>CtsUrn</code>.)</p>
