<p dir="auto">Implementation of a few special functions. So far this includes</p>
<ol dir="auto">
<li>Clausen function</li>
<li>Coulomb wave functions</li>
<li>Debye function</li>
<li>Fresnel functions</li>
<li>Struve functions</li>
<li>Hypergeometric functions</li>
<li>Confluent Hypergeometric functions</li>
<li>Fermi-Dirac Integrals</li>
</ol>
<ul dir="auto">
<li>Inverse Fermi-Dirac integrals</li>
<li>Parabolic cylinder function</li>
</ul>
