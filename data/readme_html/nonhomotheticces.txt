<p dir="auto">A small package to solve for the consumption aggregator for non-homothetic CES preferences as described in</p>
<p dir="auto"><em>Comin, D., Lashkari, D., &amp; Mestieri, Martí (2021). Structural change with long-run income and price effects. Econometrica, 89(1), 311–374.</em></p>
