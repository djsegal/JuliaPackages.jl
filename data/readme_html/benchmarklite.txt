<p dir="auto">A lightweight Julia package for simple performance benchmark.</p>
<hr>
<p dir="auto">This package is mainly for the purpose of comparing the performance of procedures (algorithms, implementations, etc).</p>

<ul dir="auto">
<li>Easy to use. (We will use a simple example to illustrate this below)</li>
<li>Very lightweight. (No dependency on any other packages. It is about 200 lines of codes.)</li>
<li>Pretty printing of results.</li>
<li>Results can be export to CSV files.</li>
</ul>
