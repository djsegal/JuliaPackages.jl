<p dir="auto">Kmers provides a specialised concrete <code>BioSequence</code> subtype, optimised for
representing short immutable sequences called kmers: contiguous sub-strings of k
nucleotides of some reference sequence.</p>
<p dir="auto">They are used extensively in bioinformatic analyses as an informational unit.
This concept was popularised by short read assemblers.
Analyses within the kmer space benefit from a simple formulation of the sampling
problem and direct in-hash comparisons.</p>
<p dir="auto">Kmers provides the type representing kmers as well as the implementations of
the APIs specified by the
<a href="https://github.com/BioJulia/BioSequences.jl"><code>BioSequences.jl</code></a> package.</p>
