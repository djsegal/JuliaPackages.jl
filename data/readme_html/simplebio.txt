Work with nucleotides:</h4>
<ul dir="auto">
<li>Function transcript (xtranscript) for transcription of DNA to RNA (xtranscript for DNA with nucleobase not ATCG.)</li>
<li>Reverse-compliment sequence using DNArc and RNArc functions.</li>
<li>Retro-Transcription of RNA to DNA using RTanscript function.</li>
<li>Function RandSeq for generating DNA, RNA or amino acid chains. rand_dna_rna function to generate a dataframe for random DNAs and their transcription.</li>
<li>Use the tranlatedna function for translation of dna sequence (also can enter RNA sequence if needed) to amino acid sequence.</li>
<li>Primer design tools (in development)</li>
</ul>
