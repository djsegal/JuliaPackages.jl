<p dir="auto"><a href="https://github.com/jump-dev/SumOfSquares.jl">SumOfSquares.jl</a> is a JuMP
extension that, when used in conjunction with <a href="https://github.com/JuliaAlgebra/MultivariatePolynomials.jl">MultivariatePolynomial</a>
and <a href="https://github.com/jump-dev/PolyJuMP.jl">PolyJuMP</a>, implements a sum of
squares reformulation for polynomial optimization.</p>
