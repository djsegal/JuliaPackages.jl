<p>A Julia wrapper for CoolProp (<a href="http://www.coolprop.org" rel="nofollow">http://www.coolprop.org</a>)</p>
<p>This is not my work, and all the credit goes to the cool <a href="https://github.com/CoolProp/CoolProp/graphs/contributors">CoolProp contributors</a>. I only put this together to make things easier for a friend of mine.</p>

<p>For development, it is possible to include a custom wrapper from <code>ENV["TestingPath"]</code>, then:</p>
<div class="highlight highlight-source-julia position-relative" data-snippet-clipboard-copy-content="ENV[&quot;includelocalwrapper&quot;]=&quot;on&quot;;

"><pre><span class="pl-c1">ENV</span>[<span class="pl-s"><span class="pl-pds">"</span>includelocalwrapper<span class="pl-pds">"</span></span>]<span class="pl-k">=</span><span class="pl-s"><span class="pl-pds">"</span>on<span class="pl-pds">"</span></span>;

<p>if <code>!haskey(ENV, "TestingPath")</code> the default <code>\test\CoolProp.jl</code> will be used.</p>
