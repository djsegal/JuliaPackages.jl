<p dir="auto">Tools for 2-body orbit propagation, Lambert problem solving, and ejection/insertion trajectory optimization. Put together as a project to get comfortable with <a href="https://julialang.org/" rel="nofollow">Julia</a>. Intended for use to plan simple missions in Kerbal Space Program.</p>
