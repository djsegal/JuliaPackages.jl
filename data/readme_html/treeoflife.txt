<p dir="auto">TreeOfLife.jl defines data types for cladograms and chronograms in phylogenetics and methods analyzing these trees.</p>
<p dir="auto">In development.</p>
<p dir="auto">Alternative packages include:</p>
<ul dir="auto">
<li><a href="https://github.com/EcoJulia/Phylo.jl">Phylo.jl</a> ("in beta", and its outdated prototype <a href="https://github.com/SabrinaJaye/Phylogenetics.jl">Phylogenetics.jl</a>)</li>
<li><a href="https://github.com/jangevaare/PhyloTrees.jl">PhyloTrees.jl</a></li>
<li><a href="https://github.com/PierreBarrat/TreeTools.jl">TreeTools.jl</a></li>
<li><a href="https://github.com/BioJulia/Phylogenies.jl">Phylogenies.jl</a> ("in development")</li>
</ul>
