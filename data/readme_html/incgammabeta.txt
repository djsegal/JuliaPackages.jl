<p dir="auto">A Julia package with functions that implement incomplete gamma and beta functions
of all kinds (upper and lower, regularized and unregularized). All functions except
inverse ones also support complex arguments.</p>
<p dir="auto">The package is written entirely in Julia and does not require any third party libraries
or additional Julia packages.</p>
