<p dir="auto">This package implements various convexification algorithms that can be used for the numerical relaxation of variational problems.</p>
<p dir="auto">Install the package with</p>
<div class="snippet-clipboard-content notranslate position-relative overflow-auto" data-snippet-clipboard-copy-content="pkg&gt; add https://github.com/koehlerson/NumericalRelaxation.jl"><pre class="notranslate"><code>pkg&gt; add https://github.com/koehlerson/NumericalRelaxation.jl
</code></pre></div>
<p dir="auto">The implemented methods are research results from the following papers:</p>
<ol dir="auto">
<li><a href="https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s00707-022-03332-1" rel="nofollow">Adaptive convexification of microsphere-based incremental damage for stress and strain softening at finite strains</a></li>
<li><a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/2211.14318" rel="nofollow">Multidimensional rank-one convexification of incremental damage models at finite strains</a></li>
</ol>
<p dir="auto">If you use one-dimensional convexification procedures, please cite the prior.
In case you want to use rank-one convexification refer to the second paper.</p>
