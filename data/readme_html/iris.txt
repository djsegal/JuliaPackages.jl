<p dir="auto">A package for computing scattering and spectral properties for Helmholtz wave operators with complex media.</p>
<p dir="auto">This is a Finite-Difference Fourier Domain solver (FDFD).</p>
<p dir="auto">In development.</p>
<p dir="auto">Below is an example of a calculation made with Iris.
It is a many-in, many-out reflectionless scattering made on a chaotic waveguide junction, as appears in our <a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/1909.04017" rel="nofollow">RSM paper</a></p>
