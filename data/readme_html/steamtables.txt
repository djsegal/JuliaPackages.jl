<p dir="auto">A Julia implementation of the IAPWS-IF97 properties of water and steam.
Provides the Gibbs and Helmholtz free energies, enthalpy, entropy, Cp, Cv and sonic velocity.</p>
<p dir="auto">Optional use of physical units via Unitful.jl</p>
<p dir="auto">Inputs are either P&amp;T, P&amp;h or P&amp;s.</p>

<p dir="auto">If unitless values are passed, the physical units listed above are assumed and the returned values will also be in these units, e.g. Kelvin for temperature, MPa for pressure.</p>
<p dir="auto">Optionally, however, you may pass Unitful.jl Quantity values, e.g. 100u"°C". The returned values will still be in the specified units, but may be easily converted via Unitful's <code>uconvert()</code></p>
