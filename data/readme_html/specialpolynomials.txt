<p>A package providing various polynomial types (assuming different
polynomial bases) beyond the standard basis polynomials in the
<code>Polynomials</code> package. This package includes many classic orthogonal
polynomials, the Lagrange and Newton interpolating polynomials, and the
Bernstein polynomials.</p>
