<p>Gen: A General-Purpose Probabilistic Programming System with Programmable Inference</p>
<p><em>Warning: This is rapidly evolving research software.</em></p>
<p>See <a href="https://gen.dev/" rel="nofollow">https://gen.dev</a> for introduction, documentation, and tutorials.</p>
<p>The Gen PLDI 2019 paper is publicly available <a href="https://dl.acm.org/citation.cfm?id=3314642" rel="nofollow">here</a>.</p>
