<p dir="auto"><strong>HighDimPDE.jl</strong> is a Julia package to <strong>solve Highly Dimensional non-local, non-linear PDEs</strong> of the form</p>
<p dir="auto"><math-renderer class="js-display-math" style="display: block" data-static-url="https://github.githubassets.com/static" data-run-id="1b38d65e1e8578f1b02b160d2aedeaa4">$$
\begin{aligned}
(\partial_t u)(t,x) &amp;= \int_{\Omega} f\big(t,x,{\bf x}, u(t,x),u(t,{\bf x}), ( \nabla_x u )(t,x ),( \nabla_x u )(t,{\bf x} ) \big) d{\bf x} \\
&amp; \quad + \big\langle \mu(t,x), ( \nabla_x u )( t,x ) \big\rangle + \tfrac{1}{2} \text{Trace} \big(\sigma(t,x) [ \sigma(t,x) ]^* ( \text{Hess}_x u)(t, x ) \big).
\end{aligned}
$$</math-renderer></p>
<p dir="auto">where <math-renderer class="js-inline-math" style="display: inline" data-static-url="https://github.githubassets.com/static" data-run-id="1b38d65e1e8578f1b02b160d2aedeaa4">$u \colon [0,T] \times \Omega \to \mathbb{R}, \Omega \subseteq \mathbb{R}^{d}$</math-renderer> is subject to initial and boundary conditions, and where <math-renderer class="js-inline-math" style="display: inline" data-static-url="https://github.githubassets.com/static" data-run-id="1b38d65e1e8578f1b02b160d2aedeaa4">$d$</math-renderer> is large.</p>
