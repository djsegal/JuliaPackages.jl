<p>A Julia package containing motion planning code related to the following papers on robotic motion planning:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="http://arxiv.org/abs/1306.3532" rel="nofollow">Fast Marching Tree: a Fast Marching Sampling-Based Method for Optimal Motion Planning in Many Dimensions</a></li>
<li><a href="http://arxiv.org/abs/1403.2483" rel="nofollow">Optimal Sampling-Based Motion Planning under Differential Constraints: the Driftless Case</a></li>
<li><a href="http://arxiv.org/abs/1405.7421" rel="nofollow">Optimal Sampling-Based Motion Planning under Differential Constraints: the Drift Case with Linear Affine Dynamics</a></li>
<li><a href="http://arxiv.org/abs/1504.08053" rel="nofollow">Monte Carlo Motion Planning for Robot Trajectory Optimization Under Uncertainty</a></li>
<li><a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/1609.05399" rel="nofollow">Evaluating Trajectory Collision Probability through Adaptive Importance Sampling for Safe Motion Planning</a></li>
</ul>
