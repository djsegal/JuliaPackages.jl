<p dir="auto">Microbiome.jl is a package for manipulating and analyzing
microbiome and microbial community data.</p>

<p dir="auto">To use the latest version of <code>Microbiome.jl</code>,
you must be on <code>julia</code> v1.6 or greater.</p>
<p dir="auto">Install <code>Microbiome.jl</code> from the Julia REPL:</p>
