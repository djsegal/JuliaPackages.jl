<ul dir="auto">
<li>Global alignment (Needleman-Wunsch algorithm with linear gap penalty)</li>
<li>Dotplots</li>
<li>Transcription, Reverse Complement, Translation, Open reading frames (ORFs)</li>
<li>GC-content, minimum skew</li>
<li>Protein mass, Extinction coefficient, Instability index, Isoelectric point, Grand average of hydropathicity (GRAVY) statistics of protein sequences</li>
</ul>
