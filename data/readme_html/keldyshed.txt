<p dir="auto"><a href="https://github.com/krivenko/KeldyshED.jl/actions/workflows/CI.yml"><img src="https://github.com/krivenko/KeldyshED.jl/actions/workflows/CI.yml/badge.svg" alt="CI" style="max-width: 100%;"></a></p>
<p dir="auto">Equilibrium Exact Diagonalization solver for finite fermionic models that can
also compute Green's functions on the Keldysh contour.</p>
<p dir="auto">Copyright (C) 2019-2023 Igor Krivenko <a href="mailto:igor.s.krivenko@gmail.com">igor.s.krivenko@gmail.com</a></p>
<p dir="auto">Copyright (C) 2015 P. Seth, I. Krivenko, M. Ferrero and O. Parcollet</p>
<p dir="auto">This is my first attempt at writing Julia code and, to a large extent, a
simplified port of the <code>TRIQS/atom_diag</code> library.</p>
<p dir="auto">Special thanks to Joseph Kleinhenz for reviewing my code, as well as for writing
the <a href="https://github.com/kleinhenz/Keldysh.jl">Keldysh.jl</a> library, which <code>KeldyshED.jl</code>
depends on.</p>
