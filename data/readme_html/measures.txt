<p>This library generalizes and unifies the notion of measures used in
<a href="https://github.com/GiovineItalia/Compose.jl">Compose</a>,
<a href="https://github.com/rohitvarkey/Compose3D.jl">Compose3D</a>,
and <a href="https://github.com/shashi/Escher.jl">Escher</a>.</p>
<p>It allows building up and representing expressions involving differing types of
units that are then evaluated, resolving them into absolute units.</p>
