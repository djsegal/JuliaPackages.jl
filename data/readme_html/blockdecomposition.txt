<p>BlockDecomposition is a package providing features to take advantage of the shape of block structured problem; in other words, problems on which Dantzig-Wolfe decomposition or Benders decomposition can be applied.</p>
<p>Although BlockDecomposition is a JuMP extension,
it is not written nor maintained by the primary developers of JuMP.</p>
