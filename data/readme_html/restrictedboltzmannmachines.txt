<p>Restricted and Deep Boltzmann Machines in Julia. Related packages:</p>
<p><a href="https://github.com/dfdx/Boltzmann.jl">https://github.com/dfdx/Boltzmann.jl</a></p>
<p><a href="https://github.com/stefan-m-lenz/BoltzmannMachines.jl">https://github.com/stefan-m-lenz/BoltzmannMachines.jl</a></p>
<p>In contrast to those packages, here we adopt a more generic approach that allows defining any kind of unit potentials (such as ReLU units, which are not included in those two packages).</p>
