<p><code>AlgorithmsCollection.jl</code> is a reimplementation of classic computer algorithms in <code>JULIA</code>. The main focus is to combine and highlight the three major benefits of <code>JULIA</code>:</p>
<ol>
<li>Functionality</li>
<li>Performance</li>
<li>Maintainability</li>
</ol>
<p>Consequently, more pure <code>JULIA</code> and less <code>import</code>.</p>
