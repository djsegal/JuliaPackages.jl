<p dir="auto">Redirect stdout and stderr to a <code>String</code>.</p>
<p dir="auto">There are other packages like this. Some have more features. I like this interface.</p>
<p dir="auto">See docstrings for <code>stdout_string, with_redirect, redirect_string</code>.</p>
<p dir="auto">The simplest, easiest function is <code>stdout_string</code>.</p>
