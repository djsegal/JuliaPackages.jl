<p dir="auto"><code>Mill.jl</code> (Multiple Instance Learning Library) is a library built on top of <a href="https://fluxml.ai" rel="nofollow"><code>Flux.jl</code></a> aimed to prototype flexible multi-instance learning models. It is developed to be:</p>
<ul dir="auto">
<li><strong>flexible</strong> and <strong>versatile</strong></li>
<li>as <strong>general</strong> as possible</li>
<li><strong>fast</strong></li>
<li>and dependent on only handful of other packages</li>
</ul>
<p dir="auto"><a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Bf0CvltIDbE" rel="nofollow"><strong>Watch our introductory talk from JuliaCon 2021</strong> </a></p>
