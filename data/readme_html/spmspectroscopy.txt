<p dir="auto">A julia library to analyze scanning tunneling and atomic force spectroscopy data.</p>
<p dir="auto"><em>Currently in early stages, with support for Nanonis spectroscopy files. So far, it is only tested for Bias and Z spectroscopy experiments. Reading of other experiments and file formats will be implemented if needed.</em></p>
