<p>This package provides high-performance implementations of Kriging estimators introduced by
<a href="https://books.google.com/books/about/The_Theory_of_Regionalized_Variables_and.html?id=TGhGAAAAYAAJ" rel="nofollow">Matheron 1971</a>,
and further developed by various geostatisticians. As the most general form of estimation with covariance/variogram
models, Kriging does <strong>not</strong> require distributional assumptions, is well-defined in general Hilbert spaces, and is
unbiased. These properties make Kriging a quite useful method for estimation with real world data.
Currently, the following Kriging variants are implemented:</p>
<ul>
<li>Simple Kriging</li>
<li>Ordinary Kriging</li>
<li>Universal Kriging</li>
<li>External Drift Kriging</li>
</ul>
