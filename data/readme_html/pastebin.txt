<p dir="auto">Pastebin.jl was originally developed by Mark Molnar,
<a href="https://github.com/molnarmark/Pastebin.jl">https://github.com/molnarmark/Pastebin.jl</a></p>
<p dir="auto">See also <a href="https://github.com/cossio/Pasteee.jl">Pasteee.jl</a>, a Julia wrapper for the <a href="https://paste.ee/" rel="nofollow">Paste.ee</a> API.
Note that Pastebin has some limitations, such as 10 pastes / day for guests accounts (see <a href="https://pastebin.com/faq#11a" rel="nofollow">https://pastebin.com/faq#11a</a>).
Paste.ee doesn't have such strict limitations.</p>
