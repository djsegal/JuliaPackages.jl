<p dir="auto">This Julia package implements some tools to work with and around generalized gamma convolutions and their multivariate extensions.</p>
<p dir="auto">A non-exhaustive list of included features:</p>
<ul dir="auto">
<li>Random number generation and infrastructure through overloading of Distributions.jl functions.</li>
<li>Density evaluation through Moschopoulos, Mathai, and novel Laguerre series in univariate case</li>
<li>Density evaluation in the multivariate case through Laguerre series</li>
<li>Laverny's Estimation through an L2 loss on density in the Laguerre basis</li>
<li>Tool sto estimate and work with k-statistics and shifted cumulants and moments, including a formal expansion to compute their formulas.</li>
<li>Estimation through a shifted-cumulant-based loss</li>
<li>Miles, Furman and Kuznetsov projection algorithm</li>
<li>...</li>
</ul>
<p dir="auto">This is still a WIP and the documentation is sparse, as are the test coverage. Use with caution.</p>
