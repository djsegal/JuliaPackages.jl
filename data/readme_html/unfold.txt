<p dir="auto">Toolbox to perform linear regression on biological signals.</p>
<p dir="auto"><a href="https://unfoldtoolbox.github.io/Unfold.jl/dev" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://camo.githubusercontent.com/7fcec4b2d3ab291529fce8ef6a4fcd4129a0683b2f5d5fe2f5c648f02db8b616/68747470733a2f2f696d672e736869656c64732e696f2f62616467652f646f63732d6465762d626c75652e737667" alt="Docs" data-canonical-src="https://img.shields.io/badge/docs-dev-blue.svg" style="max-width: 100%;"></a>
<a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" href="https://github.com/unfoldtoolbox/Unfold.jl/workflows/CI/badge.svg"><img src="https://github.com/unfoldtoolbox/Unfold.jl/workflows/CI/badge.svg" alt="" style="max-width: 100%;"></a></p>
<p dir="auto">This tool can model event related time series with mass-univariate linear (mixed) models, with optional non-linear effects and overlap correction.</p>
<p dir="auto">This kind of modelling is also known as encoding modeling, linear deconvolution, Temporal Response Functions (TRFs), linear system identification, and probably under other names. fMRI models with HRF-basis functions are also supported.</p>

<ol dir="auto">
<li>We recommend to write a Literate.jl document and place it in <code>docs/_literate/FOLDER/FILENAME.jl</code> with <code>FOLDER</code> being <code>HowTo</code>, <code>Explanation</code>, <code>Tutorial</code> or <code>Reference</code> (<a href="https://documentation.divio.com/" rel="nofollow">recommended reading on the 4 categories</a>).</li>
<li>Literate.jl converts the <code>.jl</code> file to a <code>.md</code> automatically and places it in <code>doc/src/_literate/FILENAME.jl</code>.</li>
<li>Edit <a href="https://github.com/unfoldtoolbox/Unfold.jl/blob/main/docs/make.jl">make.jl</a> with a reference to <code>doc/src/_literate/FILENAME.jl</code></li>
</ol>
