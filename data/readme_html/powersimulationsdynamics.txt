<p dir="auto"><code>PowerSimulationsDynamics.jl</code> is a Julia package for power system modeling and simulation of Power Systems dynamics. The objectives of the package are:</p>
<ul dir="auto">
<li>
<p dir="auto">Provide a flexible modeling framework that can accommodate different device models according to modeling needs.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto">Streamline the construction of large scale differential equations problems to avoid repetition of work when adding/modifying model details.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto">Exploit Julia's capabilities to improve computational performance of large scale power system dynamic simulations.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto">Provide State-of-Art modeling to assess Low-Inertia Power Systems.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p dir="auto">Check the <a href="https://github.com/NREL-Sienna/PowerSimulationsDynamics.jl/projects/1">Project Section</a> to see the pipelines of new models to be added.</p>
