<p dir="auto">Interface for modular, flexible, invertible featurization of atomic structures for machine learning purposes.</p>
<p dir="auto"><a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=la9asuZzVjU" rel="nofollow">Check out our JuliaCon 2021 talk here!</a> (note that some syntax is out of date)</p>
<p dir="auto">This package is currently focused on bulk systems. We have plans for more extensive support of organic molecules, but at the moment, <a href="https://github.com/mojaie/MolecularGraph.jl">MolecularGraph</a> is probably your best bet. PubChem stores many molecular features for the compounds they catalog, and their data can be accessed via <a href="https://github.com/JuliaHealth/PubChemCrawler.jl">PubChemCrawler</a>.</p>
