<p dir="auto">Concise algorithms written in Julia and formatted with <a href="https://carbon.now.sh/" rel="nofollow">Carbon</a>.</p>
<p dir="auto">Algorithms for machine learning, optimization, reinforcement learning, online planning, decision making under uncertainty, and sorting. All implementations are working and self-contained; refer to the <a href="./test/">test cases</a>.</p>
<blockquote>
<p dir="auto"><em>Note, these are primarily for academic purposes and are not designed for real-world usage. There are many other Julia packages that implement more sound versons of these algorithms.</em></p>
</blockquote>
