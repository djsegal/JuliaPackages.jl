<p dir="auto">In potential theory, the equilibrium measure is the limiting distribution of charges in a potential field that minimises energy, see <a href="https://www.springer.com/gp/book/9783540570783" rel="nofollow">Saff and Totic 1997</a> and <a href="https://bookstore.ams.org/cln-3" rel="nofollow">Deift 1999</a>. These distributions also describe the distribution of</p>
<ol dir="auto">
<li>Zeros of orthogonal polynomials</li>
<li>Fekete (near optimal interpolation) points</li>
<li>Eigenvalues of random matrices
This package facilitates their computation, and is based on <a href="https://github.com/dlfivefifty/RHPackage">RHPackage</a>.</li>
</ol>
