<p dir="auto">This small package defines the tropical semi-ring with either the max or min convention.
With the max convention this is the semi-ring (ℝ ∪ {-∞}, ⊕, ⊙) where
⊕ is the usual multiplication and ⊙ is the usual maximum.
With the min convention this is the semi-ring (ℝ ∪ {∞}, ⊕, ⊙) where
⊕ is the usual multiplication and ⊙ is the usual minimum.
This corresponding Julia type is</p>
<div class="highlight highlight-source-julia notranslate position-relative overflow-auto" dir="auto" data-snippet-clipboard-copy-content="Trop{MM&lt;:Union{Min, Max}, T&lt;:Real} &lt;: Number"><pre>Trop{MM<span class="pl-k">&lt;:</span><span class="pl-c1">Union{Min, Max}</span>, T<span class="pl-k">&lt;:</span><span class="pl-c1">Real</span>} <span class="pl-k">&lt;:</span> <span class="pl-c1">Number</span></pre></div>
