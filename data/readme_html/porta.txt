<p>Please orient yourself through <a href="https://theportal.wiki/wiki/Graph,_Wall,_Tome" rel="nofollow">The Portal - Unofficial Wiki</a>! The ideal state would be a tool that you could use for visualizing and inspecting the equations on the famous wall, located at <a href="http://www.math.stonybrook.edu/~tony/scgp/wall-story/wall-story.html" rel="nofollow">Stony Brook University</a>.
