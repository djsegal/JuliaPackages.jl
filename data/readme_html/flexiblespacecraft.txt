<p dir="auto"><code>FlexibleSpacecraft.jl</code> is <strong>an Open Source Spacecraft Attitude-Structure Coupling Simulator and Evaluator</strong> developed in Julia Language, aiming to accelerate the development of the challenging space missions.</p>
<p dir="auto">Our focus is the coupling problem of structural vibration and spacecraft attitude. The structural and attitude control systems of spacecraft have been treated as separate problems. However, the coupled structure-attitude dynamic analysis will be necessary for the next generation spacecraft with extensive and flexible structures envisioned in the future. A simulator of the structure-attitude coupling system will make it possible to verify the problem efficiently and accelerate the development of the next-generation spacecraft.</p>
<p dir="auto"><em>This project is quite new and under active development for open-source release.</em></p>
