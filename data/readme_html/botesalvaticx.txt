<p>Implements the Bote-Salvat ionization cross section model described in</p>
<ul>
<li>D. Bote and F. Salvat, <em>"Calculations of inner-shell ionization by electron impact with the distorted-wave and plane-wave Born approximations"</em>, Phys. Rev. <strong>A77</strong>, 042701 (2008).</li>
<li>Bote, David, et al. <em>"Cross sections for ionization of K, L and M shells of atoms by impact of electrons and positrons with energies up to 1 GeV: Analytical formulas."</em> Atomic Data and Nuclear Data Tables <strong>95.6</strong> (2009): 871-909.</li>
</ul>
<p>This is a very lean implementation that only handles electrons.</p>
<p>Elements are identified by atomic number, <code>z</code>.</p>
<p>Sub-shells are identified by integer indices where 1-&gt;K, 2-&gt;L₁, 3-&gt;L₂, ...,9-&gt;M₅ (IUPAC notation) or
1-&gt;1S½, 2-&gt;2S½, 3-&gt;2P½, 4-&gt;2P³/₂,... (atomic notation).</p>
<div class="highlight highlight-source-julia position-relative" data-snippet-clipboard-copy-content="ionizationcrosssection(z::Int, subshell::Int, energy::AbstractFloat, edgeenergy::AbstractFloat=edgeenergy(z, subshell))
"><pre><span class="pl-c1">ionizationcrosssection</span>(z<span class="pl-k">::</span><span class="pl-c1">Int</span>, subshell<span class="pl-k">::</span><span class="pl-c1">Int</span>, energy<span class="pl-k">::</span><span class="pl-c1">AbstractFloat</span>, edgeenergy<span class="pl-k">::</span><span class="pl-c1">AbstractFloat</span><span class="pl-k">=</span><span class="pl-c1">edgeenergy</span>(z, subshell))</pre></div>
<ul>
<li>Computes the cross-section in square centimeters for z=1:99, subshell=1:&lt;=9, energy = 0 to 1 GeV in eV</li>
<li>If edgeenergy is nothing, the B-S recommended value is used, otherwise the user may provide an edge energy in eV</li>
<li>Returns 0.0 if energy&lt;edgeenergy</li>
<li>Throws an assertion if data isn't available for the specified element and sub-shell.</li>
</ul>
