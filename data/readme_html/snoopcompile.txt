<p>SnoopCompile observes the Julia compiler, causing it to record the
functions and argument types it's compiling.  From these lists of methods,
you can generate lists of <code>precompile</code> directives that may reduce the latency between
loading packages and using them to do "real work."</p>
<p>See the documentation:</p>
