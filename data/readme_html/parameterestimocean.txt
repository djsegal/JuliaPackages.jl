<p dir="auto">A Julia package that leverages <a href="http://github.com/CliMA/Oceananigans.jl/">Oceananigans.jl</a> and <a href="https://github.com/CliMA/EnsembleKalmanProcesses.jl">EnsembleKalmanProcesses.jl</a> to calibrate ocean turbulence parametrizations.</p>
<p dir="auto">ParameterEstimocean.jl is developed by the <a href="https://clima.caltech.edu" rel="nofollow">Climate Modeling Alliance</a> and heroic external collaborators.</p>

<div class="highlight highlight-source-julia notranslate position-relative overflow-auto" dir="auto" data-snippet-clipboard-copy-content="julia&gt; ]
