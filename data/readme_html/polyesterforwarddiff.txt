<p dir="auto">PolyesterForwardDiff.jl is a parallelized ForwardDiff.jl. That's it. It's super fast. See <a href="https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/2021.11.15.468697v1.abstract" rel="nofollow">this paper for some benchmark results</a>.</p>
