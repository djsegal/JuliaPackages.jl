<p dir="auto">A package to find isolating intervals for the real roots of a square free polynomial.</p>
<p dir="auto">The algorithm used is based on:</p>
<p dir="auto"><em>Computing Real Roots of Real Polynomials ... and now For Real!</em>
by Alexander Kobel, Fabrice Rouillier, Michael Sagraloff
<a href="1605.00410">arXiv</a> <a href="https://doi.org/10.1145/2930889.2930937" rel="nofollow">DOI</a></p>
<p dir="auto">More detail on the algorithm is found in:</p>
<p dir="auto">Computing real roots of real polynomials
Michael Sagraloff, Kurt Mehlhorn
<a href="https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jsc.2015.03.004" rel="nofollow">DOI</a></p>
</article></div>
