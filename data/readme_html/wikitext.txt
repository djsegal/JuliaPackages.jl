<p dir="auto">WikiText.jl provides an interface to the <a href="https://blog.einstein.ai/the-wikitext-long-term-dependency-language-modeling-dataset/" rel="nofollow">WikiText Long Term Dependency Language Modeling dataset</a>.</p>

<p dir="auto">WikiText exports the following 4 types, corresponding to the 4 available datasets:</p>
<ul dir="auto">
<li><code>WikiText2</code></li>
<li><code>WikiText103,</code></li>
<li><code>WikiText2Raw</code></li>
<li><code>WikiText103Raw</code></li>
</ul>
<p dir="auto">Wikitext also exports following 3 functions:</p>
<ul dir="auto">
<li><code>trainfile</code></li>
<li><code>validationfile</code></li>
<li><code>testfile</code></li>
</ul>
<p dir="auto">Downloading and unzipping the datasets will happen automatically (with your approval) when you access them for the first time, courtesy of <a href="https://github.com/oxinabox/DataDeps.jl">DataDeps.jl</a>.</p>
