<p dir="auto">A package that standardizes manipulation of CMS Certification Numbers in Julia.</p>
<p dir="auto">CMS Certification Numbers (CCNs) uniquely identify health care providers and suppliers who interact with the United States Medicare and Medicaid programs, run out of the Centers of Medicare and Medicaid Services (CMS). CCNs are standardized in the <a href="https://www.cms.gov/Regulations-and-Guidance/Guidance/Manuals/Internet-Only-Manuals-IOMs-Items/CMS1201984" rel="nofollow"><em>State Operations Manual</em>, CMS publication number 100-07</a>.</p>
