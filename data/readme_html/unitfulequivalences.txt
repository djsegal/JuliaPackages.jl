<p>This package extends the <a href="https://github.com/PainterQubits/Unitful.jl">Unitful.jl</a> package to enable conversion between quantities of different dimensions, related by an equivalence (e.g., conversion between mass and energy using the mass–energy equivalence <em>E</em> = <em>mc</em>²).
For its usage, see the <a href="https://sostock.github.io/UnitfulEquivalences.jl/stable" rel="nofollow">documentation</a>.</p>
