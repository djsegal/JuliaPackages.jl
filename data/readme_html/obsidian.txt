<p dir="auto">Work with <a href="https://obsidian.md" rel="nofollow">Obsidian vaults</a> in  Julia.</p>
<p dir="auto">An Obsidian vault is just a directory with markdown content. In addition to standard Markdown, the Obsidian app supports wiki-style links for navigating within your vault, and tags beginning with <code>#</code> to add metadata to your notes.</p>
<p dir="auto">But javascript is not my favorite language, so <code>Obsidian.jl</code> indexes your vault in much the same way as the Obsidian app:  you can find incoming and outgoing links to pages,  tags occurring on pages or all tags on a page.</p>
<p dir="auto">In addition, the <a href="https://github.com/blacksmithgu/obsidian-dataview">dataview plugin</a> adds a notation for key-value pairs. By default, <code>Obsidian.jl</code> also  indexes these k-v pairs.</p>
<p dir="auto">To see how it works, refer to the <a href="https://neelsmith.github.io/Obsidian.jl/stable/" rel="nofollow">documentation</a>.</p>
