<p dir="auto"><em>A package for fast help and snippets, for when you don't want to search the docs.</em></p>
<p dir="auto">TLDR means Too Long, Didn't Read. This package is inspired by <a href="https://tldr.sh" rel="nofollow">tldr-pages</a>, but focused on Julia. They probably don't overlap.</p>
<p dir="auto"><strong>Work in progress, let me know your opinion</strong></p>
<hr>
<p dir="auto">I have created a proof-of-concept of the search online: <a href="https://abelsiqueira.github.io/TLDR.jl/" rel="nofollow">https://abelsiqueira.github.io/TLDR.jl/</a></p>
