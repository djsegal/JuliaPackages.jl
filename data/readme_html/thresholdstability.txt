<p dir="auto">To install, use</p>
<div class="highlight highlight-source-julia notranslate position-relative overflow-auto" dir="auto" data-snippet-clipboard-copy-content="Pkg.add(&quot;ThresholdStability&quot;)"><pre>Pkg<span class="pl-k">.</span><span class="pl-c1">add</span>(<span class="pl-s"><span class="pl-pds">"</span>ThresholdStability<span class="pl-pds">"</span></span>)</pre></div>

<div class="highlight highlight-source-julia notranslate position-relative overflow-auto" dir="auto" data-snippet-clipboard-copy-content="] add ThresholdStability"><pre>] add ThresholdStability</pre></div>
