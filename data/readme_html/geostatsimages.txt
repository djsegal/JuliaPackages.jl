<p><em>Training images for geostastical simulation in Julia.</em></p>
<p><a href="https://github.com/JuliaEarth/GeoStatsImages.jl/actions"><img src="https://camo.githubusercontent.com/18855928008b88a85be08b09737c4544d793681f4a9a480aef0098523a462f78/68747470733a2f2f696d672e736869656c64732e696f2f6769746875622f776f726b666c6f772f7374617475732f4a756c696145617274682f47656f5374617473496d616765732e6a6c2f4349" alt="" data-canonical-src="https://img.shields.io/github/workflow/status/JuliaEarth/GeoStatsImages.jl/CI" style="max-width:100%;"></a> <a href="https://codecov.io/gh/juliohm/GeoStatsImages.jl" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://camo.githubusercontent.com/cd55ddb716b1c1e8dfd6d38108c256bd41df7d82f58ff5f3a0210c23429a73e5/68747470733a2f2f636f6465636f762e696f2f67682f6a756c696f686d2f47656f5374617473496d616765732e6a6c2f6272616e63682f6d61737465722f67726170682f62616467652e737667" alt="" data-canonical-src="https://codecov.io/gh/juliohm/GeoStatsImages.jl/branch/master/graph/badge.svg" style="max-width:100%;"></a></p>
<p>This package converts famous training images from the geostatistcs
literature to a standard format for quick experimentation in Julia.
It is part of the <a href="https://github.com/juliohm/GeoStats.jl">GeoStats.jl</a>
framework and can be used in conjunction with multiple-point simulation
solvers.</p>
<p><strong>The author does not hold any copyright on the data. Please give credit to the sources in the table.</strong></p>
