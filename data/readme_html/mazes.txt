<p dir="auto"><a href="https://travis-ci.com/scheinerman/Mazes.jl" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://camo.githubusercontent.com/aaef8dd9a2a11791ddc3ac0066c7b722ec2e6495fbf9799da9c6dd876effb2ef/68747470733a2f2f7472617669732d63692e636f6d2f73636865696e65726d616e2f4d617a65732e6a6c2e7376673f6272616e63683d6d6173746572" alt="Build Status" data-canonical-src="https://travis-ci.com/scheinerman/Mazes.jl.svg?branch=master" style="max-width: 100%;"></a></p>
<p dir="auto">This is a Julia module to create mazes. Begin with <code>using Mazes</code>.</p>
<p dir="auto">A new (random) maze is created with <code>Maze(r,c)</code> where <code>r</code> and <code>c</code> are integers that
are at least two.</p>
<p dir="auto">To see the maze on the screen, use <code>draw(M)</code>. Then, to see the solution to the
maze, use <code>draw_ans(M)</code>.</p>
