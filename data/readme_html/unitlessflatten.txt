<p>UnitlessFlatten.jl extends <a href="https://github.com/rafaqz/Flatten.jl">Flatten.jl</a> to
provide struct flattening that strips units. This can be far more efficient than
simply stripping them afterwards as it improves type stability.</p>
<p>Simply use as you would use Flatten.jl, and types will be flattened to Unitless tuples
or vectors, and reconstructed including the original units.</p>
