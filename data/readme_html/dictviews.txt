<p>Provides the immutable types <code>KeysView{K,V}</code> and <code>ValuesView{K,V}</code> for dynamic, low-overhead views into the entries (either the keys or the values) of a dictionary. The types implement the <code>start</code>, <code>done</code>, and <code>next</code> methods for iterating over the entries. They respectively inherit the <code>View{K}</code> and <code>View{V}</code> abstract type which implements the <code>eltype</code>, and <code>length</code> methods.</p>
