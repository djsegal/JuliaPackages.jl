<p dir="auto"><code>GenomicVectors</code> is an alternate implementation of the <code>GPos</code> and <code>GenomicRanges</code> types from
Bioconductor's GenomicRanges package by P. Aboyoun, H. Pages and M. Lawrence. These <code>GenomicPositions</code> and
<code>GenomicRanges</code> types are <code>Vectors</code> that serve as markers of locations on a given genome. They can be used
independently or as indices and/or annotation on other objects.</p>
