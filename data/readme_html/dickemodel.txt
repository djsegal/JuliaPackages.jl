<p dir="auto"><em>A toolkit for the quantum and classical Dicke model in Julia.</em></p>
<p dir="auto">This package contains numerical methods that allow to efficiently compute properties of the quantum and classical <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dicke_model" rel="nofollow">Dicke model</a>, a fundamental model in quantum optics describing atoms interacting with light.</p>
