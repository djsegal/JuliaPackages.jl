<p dir="auto"><a href="https://github.com/lucifer1004/Wigxjpf.jl">Wigxjpf.jl</a> is an unopinionated wrapper of <a href="http://fy.chalmers.se/subatom/wigxjpf/" rel="nofollow">WIGXJPF</a>, which evaluates Wigner 3j, 6j and 9j symbols accurately using prime factorisation and multi-word integer arithmetic.</p>
