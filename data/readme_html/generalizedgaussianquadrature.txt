<p dir="auto">Gaussian quadrature is a numerical integration technique integrating polynomials up to order <math-renderer class="js-inline-math" style="display: inline" data-static-url="https://github.githubassets.com/static" data-run-id="cd51a5d42f0b975a261315d62cf50c7b">$2n-1$</math-renderer>, where <math-renderer class="js-inline-math" style="display: inline" data-static-url="https://github.githubassets.com/static" data-run-id="cd51a5d42f0b975a261315d62cf50c7b">$n$</math-renderer> is the order of the quadrature rule, exactly. Generalized gaussian quadrature extends this technique to integrate square-root functions and functions with endpoint-singularities exactly.</p>
