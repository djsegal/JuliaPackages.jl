<p>MagnitudeDistributions is a package developed to facilitate the analysis of frequency-magnitude
distributions of earthquakes. It provides methods to:</p>
<ul>
<li>estimate the magnitude of completeness;</li>
<li>estimate the parameters of frequency-magnitude distributions, such as the b-value;</li>
<li>sample theoretical frequency-magnitude distributions.</li>
</ul>
