<p dir="auto"><strong>Unravelling the evolutionary tapestry</strong>:
<a href="https://github.com/ignacioq/Tapestree.jl">Tapestree</a> is a
<a href="http://julialang.org" rel="nofollow">Julia</a> package of phylogenetic analyses of
diversification, trait and biogeographic dynamics.</p>

<p dir="auto">For the TRIBE model:</p>
<ol dir="auto">
<li>"Trait and Range Interspecific Biogeographic Evolution" (TRIBE): which implements a joint model of trait evolution and biogeographic history as described in
Quintero, I., &amp; Landis, M. J. (2020). Interdependent phenotypic and biogeographic evolution driven by biotic interactions. Systematic biology, 69(4), 739-755. <a href="https://doi.org/10.1093/sysbio/syz082" rel="nofollow">https://doi.org/10.1093/sysbio/syz082</a></li>
</ol>
<p dir="auto">For the ESSE model:</p>
<ol start="2" dir="auto">
<li>"Environmental and State dependent Speciation and Extinction" (ESSE): which implements a joint geographic and environmental model of diversification as described in
Quintero, I., Landis, M. J., Jetz, W., &amp; Morlon, H. (2022). The build-up of the present-day tropical diversity of tetrapods. Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences, 2023. 120 (20) e2220672120. <a href="https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.2220672120" rel="nofollow">https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.2220672120</a></li>
</ol>
