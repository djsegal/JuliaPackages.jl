<p dir="auto">Reshape a text (string, or multi-line string) to have a selected width.
There's other Julia packages that do this, like <a href="https://github.com/carlobaldassi/TextWrap.jl">TextWrap.jl</a>, but <code>TextBox.jl</code> can also handle alphabets with non-standard unicode characters (e.g. characters with width &gt; 1 and right-to-left languages).</p>
