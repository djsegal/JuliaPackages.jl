<div class="highlight highlight-source-julia position-relative" data-snippet-clipboard-copy-content="setproperties(obj::MyType, patch::NamedTuple)

"><pre><span class="pl-c1">setproperties</span>(obj<span class="pl-k">::</span><span class="pl-c1">MyType</span>, patch<span class="pl-k">::</span><span class="pl-c1">NamedTuple</span>)
