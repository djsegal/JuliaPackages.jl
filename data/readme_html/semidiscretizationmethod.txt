<p>Julia package to investigate the behaviour of linear delay differential equations based on the book
<a href="http://link.springer.com/10.1007/978-1-4614-0335-7" rel="nofollow">Semi-Discretization for Time-Delay Systems (by Insperger and Stepan)</a>.</p>
<p>This package provides a tool to approximate the stability properties and stationary behaviour of linear periodic delay systems of the form:</p>

<p><a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" href="https://camo.githubusercontent.com/3a176658a3f885fc6446b37fa6ebd2b8ebd663cd55ef4444b59c4c2f86eb9537/68747470733a2f2f6c617465782e636f6465636f67732e636f6d2f6769662e6c617465783f5c646f747b5c6d61746862667b787d7d2874292673706163653b3d2673706163653b5c6d61746862667b417d2874292673706163653b5c6d61746862667b787d2874292673706163653b2b2673706163653b5c73756d5f7b6a3d317d5e672673706163653b5c6d61746862667b427d5f6a2874292673706163653b5c6d61746862667b787d28742d5c7461755f6a287429292b5c6d61746862667b637d28742924"><img src="https://camo.githubusercontent.com/3a176658a3f885fc6446b37fa6ebd2b8ebd663cd55ef4444b59c4c2f86eb9537/68747470733a2f2f6c617465782e636f6465636f67732e636f6d2f6769662e6c617465783f5c646f747b5c6d61746862667b787d7d2874292673706163653b3d2673706163653b5c6d61746862667b417d2874292673706163653b5c6d61746862667b787d2874292673706163653b2b2673706163653b5c73756d5f7b6a3d317d5e672673706163653b5c6d61746862667b427d5f6a2874292673706163653b5c6d61746862667b787d28742d5c7461755f6a287429292b5c6d61746862667b637d28742924" title="\dot{\mathbf{x}}(t) = \mathbf{A}(t) \mathbf{x}(t) + \sum_{j=1}^g \mathbf{B}_j(t) \mathbf{x}(t-\tau_j(t))+\mathbf{c}(t)$" data-canonical-src="https://latex.codecogs.com/gif.latex?\dot{\mathbf{x}}(t)&amp;space;=&amp;space;\mathbf{A}(t)&amp;space;\mathbf{x}(t)&amp;space;+&amp;space;\sum_{j=1}^g&amp;space;\mathbf{B}_j(t)&amp;space;\mathbf{x}(t-\tau_j(t))+\mathbf{c}(t)$" style="max-width:100%;"></a></p>
<p>by transforming the underlying differential equation into the mapping:</p>

<p><a href="https://www.codecogs.com/eqnedit.php?latex=\mathbf{y}_{n+1}&amp;space;=&amp;space;\mathbf{F}_n\mathbf{y}_n+\mathbf{f}_n," rel="nofollow"><img src="https://camo.githubusercontent.com/468f8c1527df319f83c69d39c54d7027f2db5dd04fadb2f83762f11e37d8ce8d/68747470733a2f2f6c617465782e636f6465636f67732e636f6d2f6769662e6c617465783f5c6d61746862667b797d5f7b6e2b317d2673706163653b3d2673706163653b5c6d61746862667b467d5f6e5c6d61746862667b797d5f6e2b5c6d61746862667b667d5f6e2c" title="\mathbf{y}_{n+1} = \mathbf{F}_n\mathbf{y}_n+\mathbf{f}_n," data-canonical-src="https://latex.codecogs.com/gif.latex?\mathbf{y}_{n+1}&amp;space;=&amp;space;\mathbf{F}_n\mathbf{y}_n+\mathbf{f}_n," style="max-width:100%;"></a></p>
<p>where 
<a href="https://www.codecogs.com/eqnedit.php?latex=n" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://camo.githubusercontent.com/c389bbb7a149afb6d6a8157dfa909396d37b57a30dca75cb23b66a850c1147fa/68747470733a2f2f6c617465782e636f6465636f67732e636f6d2f6769662e6c617465783f6e" title="n" data-canonical-src="https://latex.codecogs.com/gif.latex?n" style="max-width:100%;"></a>
is the discrete time 
<a href="https://www.codecogs.com/eqnedit.php?latex=(t_n&amp;space;=&amp;space;n&amp;space;\Delta&amp;space;t)" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://camo.githubusercontent.com/29819ee5cb9ff9b2b45c6aebd1f036b7808f7f009280d16420d3f0a96f168036/68747470733a2f2f6c617465782e636f6465636f67732e636f6d2f6769662e6c617465783f28745f6e2673706163653b3d2673706163653b6e2673706163653b5c44656c74612673706163653b7429" title="(t_n = n \Delta t)" data-canonical-src="https://latex.codecogs.com/gif.latex?(t_n&amp;space;=&amp;space;n&amp;space;\Delta&amp;space;t)" style="max-width:100%;"></a>
, 
<a href="https://www.codecogs.com/eqnedit.php?latex=\mathbf{F}_n" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://camo.githubusercontent.com/ae994c2adb8aeab6e09c7949cab6b8ae4fdd5dc94414c3d8d060cd6f76192723/68747470733a2f2f6c617465782e636f6465636f67732e636f6d2f6769662e6c617465783f5c6d61746862667b467d5f6e" title="\mathbf{F}_n" data-canonical-src="https://latex.codecogs.com/gif.latex?\mathbf{F}_n" style="max-width:100%;"></a>
is the mapping matrix constructed using 
<a href="https://www.codecogs.com/eqnedit.php?latex=\mathbf{A}(t)" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://camo.githubusercontent.com/f9f153697bb01802bb8d4c3c109f3edd3cb578c9cdc0f4ae477cbfd71018dbe5/68747470733a2f2f6c617465782e636f6465636f67732e636f6d2f6769662e6c617465783f5c6d61746862667b417d287429" title="\mathbf{A}(t)" data-canonical-src="https://latex.codecogs.com/gif.latex?\mathbf{A}(t)" style="max-width:100%;"></a>
, 
<a href="https://www.codecogs.com/eqnedit.php?latex=\mathbf{B}(t)" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://camo.githubusercontent.com/e8c9f3dc8c63e22d8cadd3494213b3da3143cda49c9c6edb5426f2d5151bb13c/68747470733a2f2f6c617465782e636f6465636f67732e636f6d2f6769662e6c617465783f5c6d61746862667b427d287429" title="\mathbf{B}(t)" data-canonical-src="https://latex.codecogs.com/gif.latex?\mathbf{B}(t)" style="max-width:100%;"></a>
and 
<a href="https://www.codecogs.com/eqnedit.php?latex=\tau_j(t)" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://camo.githubusercontent.com/a85a8c51ee6fa7ab1c6f0e834b3b1a74393e494346e0943a8a90c584cbef8c0f/68747470733a2f2f6c617465782e636f6465636f67732e636f6d2f6769662e6c617465783f5c7461755f6a287429" title="\tau_j(t)" data-canonical-src="https://latex.codecogs.com/gif.latex?\tau_j(t)" style="max-width:100%;"></a>
, while the vector 
<a href="https://www.codecogs.com/eqnedit.php?latex=\mathbf{y}_n" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://camo.githubusercontent.com/a8f9708ad62ddd8aba831d54113bb6197f9be79cc7bc1b92fda9a2f5fb54aff2/68747470733a2f2f6c617465782e636f6465636f67732e636f6d2f6769662e6c617465783f5c6d61746862667b797d5f6e" title="\mathbf{y}_n" data-canonical-src="https://latex.codecogs.com/gif.latex?\mathbf{y}_n" style="max-width:100%;"></a>
is the discretized state space vector:</p>

<p><a href="https://www.codecogs.com/eqnedit.php?latex=\mathbf{y}_n&amp;space;=&amp;space;\left(\mathbf{x}(t_n)^\top,&amp;space;\mathbf{x}(t_{n-1})^\top,\ldots,\mathbf{x}(t_{n-r})\right)^\top\!." rel="nofollow"><img src="https://camo.githubusercontent.com/aa9dac516c314a04b493c04d0831bfd4505a7f8774d1b782dbceeb8508dd167e/68747470733a2f2f6c617465782e636f6465636f67732e636f6d2f6769662e6c617465783f5c6d61746862667b797d5f6e2673706163653b3d2673706163653b5c6c656674285c6d61746862667b787d28745f6e295e5c746f702c2673706163653b5c6d61746862667b787d28745f7b6e2d317d295e5c746f702c5c6c646f74732c5c6d61746862667b787d28745f7b6e2d727d295c7269676874295e5c746f705c212e" title="\mathbf{y}_n = \left(\mathbf{x}(t_n)^\top, \mathbf{x}(t_{n-1})^\top,\ldots,\mathbf{x}(t_{n-r})\right)^\top\!." data-canonical-src="https://latex.codecogs.com/gif.latex?\mathbf{y}_n&amp;space;=&amp;space;\left(\mathbf{x}(t_n)^\top,&amp;space;\mathbf{x}(t_{n-1})^\top,\ldots,\mathbf{x}(t_{n-r})\right)^\top\!." style="max-width:100%;"></a></p>
<p>Each coefficient matrices of delay differential equations are periodic, with a principle period of 
<a href="https://www.codecogs.com/eqnedit.php?latex=P" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://camo.githubusercontent.com/65c3b5ca4c2e9b04033c942092135d8c2540081cb3df10a4da44c18f3c552a1a/68747470733a2f2f6c617465782e636f6465636f67732e636f6d2f6769662e6c617465783f50" title="P" data-canonical-src="https://latex.codecogs.com/gif.latex?P" style="max-width:100%;"></a>
, namely:</p>

<p><a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" href="https://camo.githubusercontent.com/8899e56c11943d1b14ef3291b433951f2a3b30fe3196e00a048b3238ddb571e2/68747470733a2f2f6c617465782e636f6465636f67732e636f6d2f6769662e6c617465783f412874293d4128742b50292c5c3b2673706163653b425f6a2874293d425f6a28742b50292c5c3b2673706163653b5c7461755f6a2874293d5c7461755f6a28742b502924292673706163653b616e642673706163653b24632874293d6328742b50292e"><img src="https://camo.githubusercontent.com/8899e56c11943d1b14ef3291b433951f2a3b30fe3196e00a048b3238ddb571e2/68747470733a2f2f6c617465782e636f6465636f67732e636f6d2f6769662e6c617465783f412874293d4128742b50292c5c3b2673706163653b425f6a2874293d425f6a28742b50292c5c3b2673706163653b5c7461755f6a2874293d5c7461755f6a28742b502924292673706163653b616e642673706163653b24632874293d6328742b50292e" title="A(t)=A(t+P),\; B_j(t)=B_j(t+P),\; \tau_j(t)=\tau_j(t+P)$) and $c(t)=c(t+P)." data-canonical-src="https://latex.codecogs.com/gif.latex?A(t)=A(t+P),\;&amp;space;B_j(t)=B_j(t+P),\;&amp;space;\tau_j(t)=\tau_j(t+P)$)&amp;space;and&amp;space;$c(t)=c(t+P)." style="max-width:100%;"></a></p>
Furthermore, the integer 
<a href="https://www.codecogs.com/eqnedit.php?latex=r" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://camo.githubusercontent.com/a4b7a707c3fdd5c8f77b794d08826a28f2328b69f34c664cae4cb34bc8213704/68747470733a2f2f6c617465782e636f6465636f67732e636f6d2f6769662e6c617465783f72" title="r" data-canonical-src="https://latex.codecogs.com/gif.latex?r" style="max-width:100%;"></a>
is chosen in a way, that 
<a href="https://www.codecogs.com/eqnedit.php?latex=\inline&amp;space;r\Delta&amp;space;t\geq&amp;space;\max_{t&amp;space;\in&amp;space;\left[0,P\right],j=1\ldots&amp;space;g}\tau_j(t)" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://camo.githubusercontent.com/f58424507876f544de63debd42ec2b16996483bf709425e84b456edbd917ebce/68747470733a2f2f6c617465782e636f6465636f67732e636f6d2f6769662e6c617465783f5c696e6c696e652673706163653b725c44656c74612673706163653b745c6765712673706163653b5c6d61785f7b742673706163653b5c696e2673706163653b5c6c6566745b302c505c72696768745d2c6a3d315c6c646f74732673706163653b677d5c7461755f6a287429" title="r\Delta t\geq \max_{t \in \left[0,P\right],j=1\ldots g}\tau_j(t)" data-canonical-src="https://latex.codecogs.com/gif.latex?\inline&amp;space;r\Delta&amp;space;t\geq&amp;space;\max_{t&amp;space;\in&amp;space;\left[0,P\right],j=1\ldots&amp;space;g}\tau_j(t)" style="max-width:100%;"></a>
 (the discretized "history function" contains all possible delayed values).  
<p>With the use of the discrete mapping, the stability of the original system can be investigated (approximately), by the spectral radius 
<a href="https://www.codecogs.com/eqnedit.php?latex=\rho" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://camo.githubusercontent.com/944515c6c401a572b500b7307b761e7a1d0a2cca0fbb7cbd6c1dfb863defbd4f/68747470733a2f2f6c617465782e636f6465636f67732e636f6d2f6769662e6c617465783f5c72686f" title="\rho" data-canonical-src="https://latex.codecogs.com/gif.latex?\rho" style="max-width:100%;"></a>
of the product of the mapping matrices 
<a href="https://www.codecogs.com/eqnedit.php?latex=\mathbf{F}_n" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://camo.githubusercontent.com/ae994c2adb8aeab6e09c7949cab6b8ae4fdd5dc94414c3d8d060cd6f76192723/68747470733a2f2f6c617465782e636f6465636f67732e636f6d2f6769662e6c617465783f5c6d61746862667b467d5f6e" title="\mathbf{F}_n" data-canonical-src="https://latex.codecogs.com/gif.latex?\mathbf{F}_n" style="max-width:100%;"></a>
over a period:</p>

<p><a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" href="https://camo.githubusercontent.com/9ec3aa78f8b12cf35f36e7e244a933aa091c7f980fa27b028e4e1ce45a6f0e4f/68747470733a2f2f6c617465782e636f6465636f67732e636f6d2f6769662e6c617465783f5c72686f5c6c656674285c6d61746862667b467d5f7b6e2b702d317d5c63646f745c6d61746862667b467d5f7b6e2b702d327d5c63646f745c6c646f74735c63646f745c6d61746862667b467d5f7b6e7d5c7269676874295c6c6566745c7b2673706163653b5c626567696e7b6d61747269787d2673706163653b3c312673706163653b262673706163653b5c52696768746172726f772673706163653b262673706163653b5c746578747b7468652673706163653b6d617070696e672673706163653b69732673706163653b737461626c657d5c5c2673706163653b3e312673706163653b262673706163653b5c52696768746172726f772673706163653b262673706163653b5c746578747b7468652673706163653b6d617070696e672673706163653b69732673706163653b756e737461626c657d2673706163653b5c656e647b6d61747269787d2673706163653b5c72696768742e"><img src="https://camo.githubusercontent.com/9ec3aa78f8b12cf35f36e7e244a933aa091c7f980fa27b028e4e1ce45a6f0e4f/68747470733a2f2f6c617465782e636f6465636f67732e636f6d2f6769662e6c617465783f5c72686f5c6c656674285c6d61746862667b467d5f7b6e2b702d317d5c63646f745c6d61746862667b467d5f7b6e2b702d327d5c63646f745c6c646f74735c63646f745c6d61746862667b467d5f7b6e7d5c7269676874295c6c6566745c7b2673706163653b5c626567696e7b6d61747269787d2673706163653b3c312673706163653b262673706163653b5c52696768746172726f772673706163653b262673706163653b5c746578747b7468652673706163653b6d617070696e672673706163653b69732673706163653b737461626c657d5c5c2673706163653b3e312673706163653b262673706163653b5c52696768746172726f772673706163653b262673706163653b5c746578747b7468652673706163653b6d617070696e672673706163653b69732673706163653b756e737461626c657d2673706163653b5c656e647b6d61747269787d2673706163653b5c72696768742e" title="\rho\left(\mathbf{F}_{n+p-1}\cdot\mathbf{F}_{n+p-2}\cdot\ldots\cdot\mathbf{F}_{n}\right)\left\{ \begin{matrix} &lt;1 &amp; <p>Furthermore, the stationary solution can be determined by the periodic fix point (stationary orbit) of the mapping.</p>
