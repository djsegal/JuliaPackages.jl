<p dir="auto">This package provides digital signal processing indicators developed by John F. Ehlers.</p>
<p dir="auto">Currently the original indicators shared in his book: Cycle Analytics for Traders, Advanced Technical Trading Concepts are provided with intent to explore the DSP space and provide new intuitions based on the Ehlers framework.</p>
