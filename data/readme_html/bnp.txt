<p>BNP.jl is a Julia package implementing state of the art <strong>Bayesian nonparametric</strong> models for medium-sized unsupervised problems.
The software package brings Bayesian nonparametrics to non-specialists allowing the widespread use of Bayesian nonparametric models.
Emphasis is put on consistency, performance and ease of use allowing easy access to Bayesian nonparametric models inside Julia.</p>
