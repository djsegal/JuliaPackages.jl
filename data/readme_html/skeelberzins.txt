<p dir="auto">Solver for one-dimensional parabolic and elliptic nonlinear partial differential equations.</p>
<p dir="auto">This package is based on the spatial discretization for the method of lines introduced by Skeel and Berzins in [1]. For transient problems, the time discretization is performed either by the implicit Euler method (internal method) or by using an ODE/DAE solver from the <a href="https://github.com/SciML/DifferentialEquations.jl">DifferentialEquations.jl</a> package.</p>
