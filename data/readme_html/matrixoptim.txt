<p dir="auto">MILP, Robust Optim. and Stochastic Optim., Decomposition Algorithms, and more in Matrix.</p>
<p dir="auto"><a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" href="/img/tangram.png"><img src="/img/tangram.png" alt="Tangram" style="max-width: 100%;"></a></p>
<p dir="auto"><code>MatrixOptim.jl</code> is a package to model and solve optimization in uncertain context. The
templates for robust optimization and stochastic optimization formulated in matrix are very
coherent comprehensive, and the algorithms in matrix are very explicit.</p>
<p dir="auto">This is a package I developed in 2019. Don't know too much about tests and documentation
that time. I am trying to keep it up-to-date these days.</p>

<p dir="auto">For mixed integer linear programming:</p>
<div class="highlight highlight-source-julia notranslate position-relative overflow-auto" dir="auto" data-snippet-clipboard-copy-content="model = getModel(vec_c, mat_aa, vec_b)

<p dir="auto">For mixed integer linear programming with Benders decomposition:</p>
<div class="highlight highlight-source-julia notranslate position-relative overflow-auto" dir="auto" data-snippet-clipboard-copy-content="model = getModelBenders(n_x, n_y, vec_min_y, vec_max_y, vec_c, vec_f, vec_b, mat_aa, mat_bb)

<span class="pl-c1">solveModelBenders!</span>(model)</pre></div>
<p dir="auto">Right now, the supported solver is <code>GLPK</code>. Will add the feature to select other solvers,
like <code>Gurobi</code> and <code>CPLEX</code> later.</p>
