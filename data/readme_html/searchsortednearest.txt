<div class="highlight highlight-source-julia notranslate position-relative overflow-auto" dir="auto" data-snippet-clipboard-copy-content="searchsortednearest(a, x; by=&lt;transform&gt;, lt=&lt;comparison&gt;, distance=(a,b)-&gt;abs(a-b), rev=false)"><pre><span class="pl-c1">searchsortednearest</span>(a, x; by<span class="pl-k">=</span><span class="pl-k">&lt;</span>transform<span class="pl-k">&gt;</span>, lt<span class="pl-k">=</span><span class="pl-k">&lt;</span>comparison<span class="pl-k">&gt;</span>, distance<span class="pl-k">=</span>(a,b)<span class="pl-k">-&gt;</span><span class="pl-c1">abs</span>(a<span class="pl-k">-</span>b), rev<span class="pl-k">=</span><span class="pl-c1">false</span>)</pre></div>
<p dir="auto">Find the index of (sorted) collection <code>a</code> that has the smallest <code>distance</code> to <code>x</code>.<br>
Ties go to the smallest index.</p>
