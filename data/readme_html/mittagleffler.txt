<p dir="auto">Arguments must satisfy <code>α &gt; 0</code>, <code>β</code> real, <code>z</code> real or complex, <code>ρ&gt;0</code>.</p>
<p dir="auto">For <code>α&lt;1</code> and/or <code>abs(z)&lt;1</code>, accurate, series-only method are used. The series-only methods work
with BigFloat precision for corresponding input types. Some other parameter ranges also use series
or asymptotic methods.</p>
<p dir="auto">For some arguments, integrals are evaluated with <code>quadgk</code>, with no control on errors. Some results
are accurate, others are not.</p>
