<p dir="auto">To extract a color palette from an image, call <code>quantize</code> with a <a href="https://juliaimages.org/ColorQuantization.jl/dev/api/#methods_api" rel="nofollow">quantization method</a> of your choice.</p>
<div class="highlight highlight-source-julia notranslate position-relative overflow-auto" dir="auto" data-snippet-clipboard-copy-content="using ColorQuantization

<span class="pl-k">using</span> TestImages

img <span class="pl-k">=</span> <span class="pl-c1">testimage</span>(<span class="pl-s"><span class="pl-pds">"</span>peppers<span class="pl-pds">"</span></span>)

<span class="pl-c1">quantize</span>(img, <span class="pl-c1">KMeansQuantization</span>(<span class="pl-c1">256</span>))  <span class="pl-c"><span class="pl-c">#</span> quantize to 256 colors</span>
<span class="pl-c1">quantize</span>(img, <span class="pl-c1">UniformQuantization</span>(<span class="pl-c1">8</span>))   <span class="pl-c"><span class="pl-c">#</span> quantize to an 8x8x8 grid</span></pre></div>
