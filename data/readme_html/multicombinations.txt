<ul dir="auto">
<li>
<p dir="auto"><strong>multichoose</strong>(n, k)</p>
<p dir="auto">Return the number of multisets of length <code>k</code> on <code>n</code> symbols.</p>
<p dir="auto">Examples</p>
<div class="highlight highlight-source-julia notranslate position-relative overflow-auto" dir="auto" data-snippet-clipboard-copy-content="multichoose(3,2)"><pre><span class="pl-c1">multichoose</span>(<span class="pl-c1">3</span>,<span class="pl-c1">2</span>)</pre></div>
<p dir="auto">yields</p>
<div class="snippet-clipboard-content notranslate position-relative overflow-auto" data-snippet-clipboard-copy-content="6"><pre class="notranslate"><code>6

</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto"><strong>multicombinations</strong>(xs, k)</p>
<p dir="auto">Iterate over every <code>k</code>-size multisubset of a collection <code>xs</code>.</p>
<p dir="auto">Example:</p>
<div class="highlight highlight-source-julia notranslate position-relative overflow-auto" dir="auto" data-snippet-clipboard-copy-content="for i in multicombinations([1,2,3],2)
    @show i
end"><pre><span class="pl-k">for</span> i <span class="pl-k">in</span> <span class="pl-c1">multicombinations</span>([<span class="pl-c1">1</span>,<span class="pl-c1">2</span>,<span class="pl-c1">3</span>],<span class="pl-c1">2</span>)
    <span class="pl-c1">@show</span> i
<span class="pl-k">end</span></pre></div>
<p dir="auto">yields</p>
<div class="snippet-clipboard-content notranslate position-relative overflow-auto" data-snippet-clipboard-copy-content="i =&gt; [1,1]

</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto"><strong>integersums</strong>(n, k)</p>
<p dir="auto">Iterate over every nonnegative integer solution of the equation:
<em>x<sub>1</sub> + x<sub>2</sub> + ... + x<sub>n</sub> = k</em>.</p>
<p dir="auto">The order in which the solutions are given corresponds to the order of
subsets given by <code>multicombinations(xs, k)</code>.</p>
<p dir="auto">Example:</p>
<div class="highlight highlight-source-julia notranslate position-relative overflow-auto" dir="auto" data-snippet-clipboard-copy-content="for i in integersums(3,2)
    @show i
end"><pre><span class="pl-k">for</span> i <span class="pl-k">in</span> <span class="pl-c1">integersums</span>(<span class="pl-c1">3</span>,<span class="pl-c1">2</span>)
    <span class="pl-c1">@show</span> i
<span class="pl-k">end</span></pre></div>
<p dir="auto">yields</p>
<div class="snippet-clipboard-content notranslate position-relative overflow-auto" data-snippet-clipboard-copy-content="i =&gt; [2,0,0]

i =&gt; [0,0,2]
</code></pre></div>
</li>

corresponding multicombination index array.</p>
