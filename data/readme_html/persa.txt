From the Julia REPL, type <code>]</code> to enter the Pkg REPL mode and run:</p>
<div class="snippet-clipboard-content position-relative" data-snippet-clipboard-copy-content="pkg&gt; add Persa
"><pre><code>pkg&gt; add Persa
</code></pre></div>

<ul>
