<p>Based on the <strong>symbolic operator representation</strong> of a quantum lattice system in condensed matter physics that is generated by the package <a href="https://github.com/Quantum-Many-Body/QuantumLattices.jl"><code>QuantumLattices</code></a>, exact diagonalization method is implemented for fermionic, hard-core-bosonic and spin systems.</p>
