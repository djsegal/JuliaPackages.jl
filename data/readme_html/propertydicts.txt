<p dir="auto">Wrap an <code>AbstractDict</code> to add <code>getproperty</code> support for <code>Symbol</code> and <code>String</code> keys.</p>

  :bar =&gt; 2
  :foo =&gt; 1

julia&gt; d.foo, d.bar, d.&quot;foo&quot;

julia&gt; propertynames(d)
