<p dir="auto">Finally a number type that you can count with your fingers. Super Mario and Zelda would be proud.</p>
<p dir="auto">Comes in two flavours: <code>Float8</code> has 3 exponent bits and 4 fraction bits, <code>Float8_4</code> has 4 exponent bits and 3 fraction bits.
Both rely on conversion to Float32 to perform any arithmetic operation, similar to <code>Float16</code>.</p>
<p dir="auto">CAUTION: <code>Float8_4(::Float32)</code> currently contains a bug for subnormals.</p>
