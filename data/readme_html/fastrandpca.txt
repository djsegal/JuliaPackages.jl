<p dir="auto"><a href="https://codecov.io/gh/vpetukhov/FastRandPCA.jl" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://camo.githubusercontent.com/b6a753a1f3e94cd0b068bea3d8f18cc1fa1a1b708efb28d5c8e2ee71cf605d6a/68747470733a2f2f636f6465636f762e696f2f67682f76706574756b686f762f4661737452616e645043412e6a6c2f6272616e63682f6d61696e2f67726170682f62616467652e737667" alt="Coverage" data-canonical-src="https://codecov.io/gh/vpetukhov/FastRandPCA.jl/branch/main/graph/badge.svg" style="max-width: 100%;"></a></p>
<p dir="auto">Fast randomized PCA algorithms for Sparse Data. It's a julia re-implementation of Matlab <a href="https://github.com/XuFengthucs/frPCA_sparse">frPCA_sparse</a>.</p>
<p dir="auto">The package includes an implementation of two functions <code>eig_svd</code> and <code>pca</code>, which are designed to work with sparse matrices (<code>SparseMatrixCSC</code>), though can also be applied with normal ones (<code>Matrix</code>).</p>
