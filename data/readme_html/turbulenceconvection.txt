<p dir="auto">TurbulenceConvection (Single Column Atmospheric Model in Julia) provides a framework for testing parameterizations of clouds and turbulence.
It is particularly designed to support eddy-diffusivity mass-flux modeling frameworks.</p>
<p dir="auto">Information about the EDMF parameterization implemented in TurbulenceConvection can be found in:</p>
<p dir="auto">Tan, Z., Kaul, C. M., Pressel, K. G., Cohen, Y., Schneider, T., and Teixeira, J. (2018)
<strong>An extended eddy-diffusivity mass-flux scheme for unified representation of
subgrid-scale turbulence and convection.</strong> <em>Journal of Advances in Modeling Earth Systems</em>. <a href="https://doi.org/10.1002/2017MS001162" rel="nofollow">doi</a></p>
<p dir="auto">Cohen, Y., Lopez-Gomez, I., Jaruga, A., He, J., Kaul, C., and Schneider, T. (2020) <strong>Unified entrainment and detrainment closures for extended eddy-diffusivity mass-flux schemes.</strong> <em>Journal of Advances in Modeling Earth Systems</em>, 12, e2020MS002162. <a href="https://doi.org/10.1029/2020MS002162" rel="nofollow">doi</a></p>
<p dir="auto">Lopez-Gomez, I., Cohen, Y., He, J., Jaruga, A., Schneider, T. (2020) <strong>A generalized mixing length closure for eddy-diﬀusivity mass-flux schemes of turbulence and convection.</strong> <em>Journal of Advances in Modeling Earth Systems</em>, 12, e2020MS002161. <a href="https://doi.org/10.1029/2020MS002161" rel="nofollow">doi</a></p>
<p dir="auto">He, J., Cohen, Y., Lopez-Gomez, I., Jaruga, A., Schneider, T. (2021) <strong>An improved perturbation pressure closure for eddy-diffusivity mass-flux schemes</strong>. <a href="https://doi.org/10.1002/essoar.10505084.2" rel="nofollow">preprint</a></p>
<p dir="auto">The code is written in Julia, and was translated from <a href="https://github.com/CliMA/SCAMPy">SCAMPy</a> for the commit 496dad0c2438235684823511cacbf5761d6a237c.</p>

<ul dir="auto">
<li>Bomex</li>

<li>Soares</li>
<li>Rico</li>
<li>TRMM_LBA</li>
<li>ARM_SGP</li>
<li>GATE_III</li>
<li>DYCOMS_RF01</li>
<li>DYCOMS_RF02</li>
<li>GABLS</li>
</ul>
